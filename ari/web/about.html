Why we built it

📚 Native vocabulary Recognises words like Ashram, Panchayat, and Dosa as familiar — not "complex."
🏫 Curriculum-aligned Trained on NCERT (CBSE), ICSE & State Board materials so "Grade 4" means what it should.
🎯 Teacher-in-the-loop Your rating comes first — every submission makes the model smarter for all teachers.
🔒 Private by default Passages stored anonymously, never linked to you.

What it measures

PassageCheck analyses sentence length, word complexity, vocabulary richness, and syllable density — combined using an ML model trained on Indian textbooks and children's literature.
