About

Know if a passage is right for your students — instantly

Reading levels, made simple for Indian classrooms

PassageCheck helps teachers quickly find out whether a passage is appropriate for their students — no guesswork, no lookup tables.

How it works

1
Paste any passage A story, textbook paragraph, worksheet, or any text you want to check.
2
Share your own rating You pick a grade first — your expertise matters and helps improve the model.
3
See PassageCheck's answer We show the predicted grade range, calibrated for CBSE, ICSE, and State Board curricula.

What it measures

PassageCheck analyses a passage across multiple dimensions of language difficulty: sentence length, word complexity, vocabulary richness, and syllable density. These features are combined using a machine learning model trained on graded reading material from Indian textbooks and children's literature — so the predictions reflect what Indian students actually read at each grade level.
