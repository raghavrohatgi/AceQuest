# Opposites

Big or small, under skies so blue, In this wide world, both shine true Dikka Pong

Sharp and blunt, they find their space One for might, one to sit in place Mil · am Mora

Black and white, colors play, In the dark or light of day Do · me Seng · ki

Old and young, under the sun &nbsp; &nbsp; Life’s journey is for everyone Ambi Bi · sa

High and low, where winds blow In the sky’s hold or valley below Borang Nokachik

Curvy or straight, in shapes we find Some twist and turn, some are lined Adil Chig ring

Thick or thin, the notes begin &nbsp; &nbsp; Instruments play a melody within Dama Illep

Wet or dry, on the plate they lie Tasty flavors, my, oh, my Kapa Sakkin

Long and short, like a playful sport One goes far, the other’s a great support Cha · am Rimol

Broad or narrow, a delightful view &nbsp; &nbsp; All shapes and sizes, a colorful crew Ruan Me · gol
