# The Worst Best Friends

This is Lila. This is her cat.

Lila and her cat are the BEST at being the WORST together.

Lila jumps in the muddy field. The cat does, too. The cat runs among the cows, and Lila goes MOO.

Lila scratches her big brother. The cat does, too. The cat catches a lizard…

and Lila puts it in his shampoo. Lila creeps under the bed. The cat does, too.

The cat traps some beetles…

and Lila eats a few. Lila gets a stomach ache. The cat does, too.

The cat coughs up a hairball, and Lila throws up her stew.

Lila and her cat are the BEST at being the WORST together. It is absolutely true.

But the cat and Lila are always ready for Day Two.
