# Sweet Dreams

A world of dreams in Faizu’s mind, Filled with sweets of every kind. Peda, rasmalai and kheer so sweet, Oh, what a delicious treat!

First up, all hail the king of ghee: King Mysore pak! Enter, please!

Mysore pak, Mysore pak! Pointy-cornered Mysore pak! If the king is here in his darbar Can the queen herself be far?

Kaju katli, kaju katli! Shimmering-glittering kaju katli. Creamy white is this katli, Dancing a jig with jalebi.

Orange jalebi, orange jalebi! Twisting, turning orange jalebi.

How many hands has jalebi got? Like boondis in a laddoo—a lot!

Boondi laddoo, boondi laddoo! Turning, tumbling, playful laddoo.

Laddoo, is anyone as round as you? The golden gulab jamun, that’s who!

Gulab jamun, gulab jamun! Bobbing in syrup, gulab jamun! Who else swims like you? We call him the rasgulla, we do!

Dear rasgulla, sweet rasgulla! Soft and juicy rasgulla. Rolling about here, there and everywhere Halwa asks, “Are you going somewhere?”

Carrot halwa, carrot halwa! Steaming hot carrot halwa. A bite warms Faizu’s heart.

Oh no! He wakes up with a start.

Where did you go, laddoo, jamun and katli? Halwa, Mysore pak and barfi? Faizu has his eyes tightly closed,

Off to dance with the sweets he goes.
