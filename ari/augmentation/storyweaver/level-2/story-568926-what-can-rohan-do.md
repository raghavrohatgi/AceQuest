# What Can Rohan Do?

Pranam, my name is Rohan.&nbsp; I am from Timki, a village on the banks of Ganga.&nbsp; This year I started grade seven.&nbsp; As I am growing older, I am learning many things about myself.&nbsp; Does this happen to you too?

Today is Tuesday.&nbsp; On Tuesdays, I have Math classes.&nbsp; All my friends are good at Math but I find numbers very confusing. When I add two and two and when I multiply two into two the answer is the same funny four.

But when I write five into five equals ten, a teary ten looks at me confused.&nbsp; Why is ten sad? Did I do something wrong?

In class Rana Sir gives us test results. I only get . I can’t understand Math.&nbsp; I never want to do Math again.

At home, I try to solve the test again. The numbers look sad and tired. I give up. Mohan, my brother, asks me to show him how to hold the cricket bat.&nbsp; Our friend Sunil throws the ball.

I show Mohan the right way to hold the bat.&nbsp; Thak Thak Thak. I hit one six and two fours.&nbsp; ‘Yayy! Bhaiya you are so good! You have to practice with me everyday,’ says Mohan. ‘Rohan you have to be in my team this Sunday. Please please please,’ says Sunil. ‘Okay okay,’ I tell both of them.

They were right. I can’t do Math but I can play cricket. What are some other things I can do? I can also swim in the Ganga.&nbsp; Woosh Woosh Woosh I can also take care of our cows and goats.&nbsp; Moo Moo Mehh Mehh

I can climb the mountains quickly.&nbsp; Zoom Zoom Zoom I can grow Rajma for my village.&nbsp; Thud Thud Thud I feel very proud of myself.

If I can do so many things then I can also ask for help.&nbsp; Tomorrow I will ask Rana Sir to help me with Math. Then when we play cricket I will count my own runs.&nbsp; Tell me what are the things you can do?
