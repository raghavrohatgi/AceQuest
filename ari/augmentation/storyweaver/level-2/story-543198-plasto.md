# Plasto

Plastic Is Everywhere! Plasto tells us that our misuse of plastic has grown into&nbsp; a big problem. Plastics are polluting our lands, oceans&nbsp; and air. Our bodies too. Plastic has many uses in our day-to-day life. It can save&nbsp; lives, like in hospitals or in rescue missions. But not all&nbsp; plastic is good. By saying no to use-and-throw plastic&nbsp; cups, plates, forks, spoons, etc., each one of us can&nbsp; make a big difference. Plasto, the plastic-greedy monster, will stop growing if&nbsp; we practise the&nbsp; 4 Rs: reduce, reuse, recycle and refuse.
