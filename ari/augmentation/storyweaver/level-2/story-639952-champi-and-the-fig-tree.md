# Champi and the Fig Tree

Mangoes, watermelons, Oranges and bananas, I like them all.

But it is figs that I love best of all!

Cheev... cheev “Anna, Anna, come fast. Look, the baby squirrel is eating figs,” shouts Champi. “Coming, Champi!” says Anna.

“Oh look at those birds, Anna,” says Champi. “What big beaks they have!”

“They are Malabar grey hornbills,” says Anna. “They love figs very much.”

“See this, Champi,” says Anna. “Who could it be, Anna? Could it be a porcupine?” asks Champi.

“Porcupines cannot climb trees, Champi,” says Anna. “This must be a civet cat’s droppings. Both eat figs. But they are both very shy animals, and come out only at night.”

“Could that be a porcupine? Or is it a civet cat?” wonders Champi.

Champi wakes her brother up and points to the tree outside the window. “Oh… There, look, Anna. It’s a porcupine!” Anna wakes up. “Yes! It must be dinner time.”

Squirrels, hornbills, civet cats, porcupines and I, we all love figs the most!
