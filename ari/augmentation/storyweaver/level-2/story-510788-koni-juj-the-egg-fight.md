# Koni Juj: The Egg Fight

On the first day of the Rongali Bihu festival, Mamoni jumps out of bed.

“It is koni juj day, Anaideo,” she&nbsp; shouts. “I will fight many egg&nbsp; battles, and break the most&nbsp; number of eggs, just&nbsp; you watch.&nbsp; And then we will eat your famous,&nbsp; delicious&nbsp; egg bhaja!”

Mamoni chooses her eggs carefully. “One big egg… “One pointy egg… “One egg with a very thick shell.” Soon, she leaves for the koni juj.

First, Mamoni goes to Rontu’s house. “O, Rontu! I challenge you&nbsp; to a koni juj.”

Mamoni picks her sharpest egg. Tat! Tat! TAT!

Oh no! Mamoni has lost the first battle. Rontu takes Mamoni’s egg.

Next, Mamoni goes to meet Sunmi. “O, Sunmi!” she calls. “I challenge you to a koni juj.”

This time, Mamoni chooses her biggest egg. Tap! Tap! Tap!

Poor Mamoni loses her second battle. Her big egg&nbsp; has broken too! “Yahooo! Your egg is mine now!” says Sunmi.

Finally, Mamoni is at Manab’s house. “O, Manab! Will you koni juj with me?” This time, Mamoni uses the egg with the very thick shell.

“AAAAA!” Third time lucky, Mamoni has cracked Manab’s egg! But Mamoni’s egg has broken too.

“Anaideo, I did not win any battle or eggs,” says Mamoni. “And I also lost all my eggs.” Anaideo gives Mamoni a big hug.

“Mamoni,” says Anaideo. “All that matters is that you tried your best and you had fun. Look, your friends are here. They want to share their eggs with you.”

Later, Anaideo uses the eggs to make her&nbsp; famous egg bhaja for Mamoni and her friends. What a delicious Bihu lunch it is!
