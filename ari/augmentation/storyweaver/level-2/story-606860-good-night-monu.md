# Good Night, Monu

It is bedtime and Monu wishes the world “Good night!” He hugs Amma, he kisses Appa, “Sweet dreams!”&nbsp; he says,&nbsp; “ Sleep tight!” “Good nigh t, Monu.”

He sends a kiss to every star, And to the clouds, birds and trees.

He sends a wish to each flower, And to every buzzing bee.

“Sleep tight,” he says, to the shelf on the wall, And his books lined up in a row. “Rest well,” he says, to the pots and the pans, And the clothes hanging by the door. “Good night, Monu.”

“Sweet dreams,” Monu says, to the windows, To the walls, the ceiling, the floor. “Cuddle up,” he smiles, as he tucks in his toys, And “good night,” at last, to…

“Settle down,” Monu says to the monsters, As he puts each one to bed. “Close your eyes! Go to sleep!” he says, as he leaves A kiss on each monster head.

“Sweet dreams,” he says, to the whole wide world. “And sweetest dreams to me.”

“Good night, Monu.”
