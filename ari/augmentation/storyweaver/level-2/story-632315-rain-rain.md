# Rain, Rain

A cloud rolled along merrily in the sky.&nbsp; The peacock said,&nbsp; “Why don’t you rain?&nbsp; I will dance for you.”

The fish said, “Please rain.&nbsp; I need more water.”

The farmer said,&nbsp; “Please rain.&nbsp; I’ll plant seeds for the next crop.”

Raju said,&nbsp; “If you rain, I’ll sail paper boats.”

The cloud rained in a shower of shimmering drops.

The peacock spread its pretty feathers and danced.

The pond filled up.&nbsp; The fish was happy.

The farmers started planting seeds.

Splish! Splash! Splosh!&nbsp; Raju and his friends&nbsp; had a wonderful time&nbsp; in the puddles.

The cloud filled everybody’s heart with joy. It moved on. All the children waved goodbye.
