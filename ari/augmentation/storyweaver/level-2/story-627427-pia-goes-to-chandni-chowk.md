# Pia Goes to Chandni Chowk

Pia misses Nini a lot. Nini moved to Kolkata and hasn’t been to Delhi in a long time.

Nini would love to take Pia to Chandni Chowk, the place where she grew up. But she cannot&nbsp; walk around much. Pia has an idea. Off she goes to Chandni Chowk with her Ma.

Bright pink, grass green and mango yellow. Are these colours in a box of crayons? “Creamy, juicy and frozen! I know why Nini loves fruit kulfi,” says Pia. “I want some more.”

A big black kadhai sizzling with ghee. Bubble, bubble, bubble. Plop goes a twirly orange spiral. “Hot, crunchy and sweet. No wonder, Nini loves jalebis,” says Pia. “I want some more.”

Is that a cloud? Oh, it melts in the mouth. “White, fluffy and streaked with the sun. That explains why Nini loves daulat ki chaat!” says Pia. “I want some more.”

Are those slippery yellow and pink worms, on top of a creamy white puddle? “Cold, slimy and smooth. I can see why Nini loves rabri falooda,” says Pia. “I want some more.”

What does Pia bring home for Nini?

Cold kulfi. Hot jalebis. Fluffy daulat ki chaat. Creamy rabri falooda. “The best memories of Chandni Chowk,” says Nini.
