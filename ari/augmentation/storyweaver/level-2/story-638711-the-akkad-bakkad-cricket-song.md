# The Akkad Bakkad Cricket Song

Akkad bakkad, we shout and scream, Here comes our gully cricket team!

Mummy’s team wins the toss, Sweety khala’s our opening&nbsp; batter.

The other team knows what’s at stake, They bring in their best catcher.

Billoo phuphi bowls a full toss. Oh no, is our team in a fix?

We hold our breaths and pray, Oh, look—

The ball’s been hit for a six!

Ammu chechi makes lots of runs, Lakshmi Mami takes many a wicket. “No ball!” “Not out!” “Well played!” “Howzat!” Oh, how we love our gully cricket!

Akkad bakkad, what’s that in the trees? Our match is stopped by...

We pack up and&nbsp; head back home, As Dadi calls, “End of play!”

We’ll be back for a rematch, See you next Sunday!

Akkad bakkad, east or west, Gully cricket is the best.
