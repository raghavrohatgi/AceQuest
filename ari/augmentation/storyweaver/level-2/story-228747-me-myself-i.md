# Me, Myself & I

It’s me, myself. It’s I, I say! I like myself, Hip Hip Hooray!

I like myself, I do, you see.

I like myself because I am me!

I like my hair, my eyes, my skin.

I like this self that I live in.

I like my heart, I like my mind.

And if you look closely, you will find.

That parts of me are just like you...

And you...and you...

And you...and you...

I like myself and I like you too!
