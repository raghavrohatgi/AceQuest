# I Love My Planet

When I go shopping...

I do not use plastic bags but I use cloth bags instead.

I never litter. I put all my rubbish in the bin.

I recycle and sort my rubbish into different bins.

When I see other people littering...

I remind them of the right thing to do.

I always turn off the lights when I am the last person to leave the room.

I do not overfill my plate, so I do not waste food.

I try not to watch too much TV...

...and spend my time playing outside instead.

I turn off the tap while brushing my teeth, to make sure I do not waste water. Water is precious.

I remind my family and friends to re-use their bathwater to water the plants.

I give away or exchange books and toys I do not need anymore.

Instead of travelling by car, my family and I try to take the bus...

I save our trees by drawing on both sides of the paper.

I plant herbs and flowers in the garden and help to keep our earth green...

I water them to help them grow.

I make my own colours from flowers and vegetables.

We are the children of Gelsenkirchen!

and this is how we love our planet.
