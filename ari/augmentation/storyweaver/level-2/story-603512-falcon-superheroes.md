# Falcon Superheroes

On a cold but pleasant day in Tyrso village, Melam and his friends were once again happily playing Superheroes. Suddenly, Melam heard a voice he dreaded!

“Saving the world again, are we? Tiny, weak Superheroes?” It was the voice of the village bully, Bor. “What do you want, Bor?” Melam’s voice trembled.

“Huh, how childish!” Bor sneered at them. “Do you know what my friends and I do to weak creatures like you?”

“Bor, Bor, they are here!” Everyone turned to see Bor’s friends approach him. They were all excited. “The Amur Falcons have come again, Bor! Remember your plan to catch them once the sun sets?”

“We will catch as many falcons as we can today!”&nbsp; Bor announced “NO!” shouted little Melam, “You cannot hurt those birds. They are our guests in the village.&nbsp; They’re beautiful and …and …” But Bor and his friends ignored him.

Melam went home feeling defeated.&nbsp; Seeing him upset, Mei asked, “You are back early today. Did something happen?” “The Amur Falcons…” Melam sighed. “Mei, is it alright for someone to hurt the&nbsp; Amur Falcons?” he asked, turning to his&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;mother.

His mother tried to explain how everyone in the village has learnt that these birds are a blessing to them and should never be harmed. She said: &nbsp; “These birds help to eat all the insects that eat our crops.” “Tourists from all over visit our village to see the birds and because of this we have so many different people visiting us!”

She told him how their Rangbah Shnong had been training some very special geese to attack anyone who went near the place, where the falcons usually are at night.

“If Mei finds me outside at this time, she’ll really be angry but I’ve got to warn Bor not to go near the Falcons. He might be a bully but I’m a Superhero,” Melam mumbled.

On reaching the spot, he saw&nbsp; the special birds in action! “Oww! He got me on the leg!” “I’m sorry ! I’m sorry Goose!&nbsp; I promise never to hurt anyone again!”

“Did you come to laugh at me?” Bor asked in disgust and anger, as he saw Melam the next day. “No, Bor. I’m so glad you are okay. I’m also glad that the Rangbah Shnong came in time to chase away the geese,” Melam said.

Bor was silent. The wounds looked very painful. After a while, Bor spoke up. “I should not have harmed the birds. I never realized how special they are to our village.” “My mother told me how important the Amur Falcons are for our village. The Superhero squad will also let everyone else know about this,” Melam said. “Melam, can I also be a part of your Superhero squad?” Bor asked.

“ I’m going to be a Superhero just like you, Melam,” Bor said. “Of course Bor, anyone in the village can always join the Superhero Squad.”

Activity Fellow Falcon Superheroes!&nbsp; You have a mission to&nbsp; complete. Could you help this falcon regain its beautiful colours?

Glossary Amur Falcons: These are small birds belonging to the Falcon family. While they breed in Southeast Russia and Northern China, before winter sets in, they migrate in large flocks to warmer climates. Their first stop is in the North Eastern part of India, Tyrso village in Meghalaya being one of them. After about two months, they fly all the way to Africa. Mei:&nbsp; A khasi word for Mother. Rangbah Shnong:&nbsp; The traditional head of a village who heads the ' dorbar ' or the village council. They overlook the well-being of the entire village and help in implementing the laws. &nbsp;
