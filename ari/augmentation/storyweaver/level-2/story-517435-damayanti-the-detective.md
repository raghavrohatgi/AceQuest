# Damayanti the Detective

Damayanti the Detective never goes anywhere without&nbsp; her Trusty Never Dusty 3-in-1 Dabba.&nbsp; It contains a notebook and pen, a pair of binoculars,&nbsp; and a snack.

Snacking is Damayanti’s superpower. And if you are&nbsp; a good detective like Damayanti, you will know this:&nbsp; ALL good mysteries involve snacks.

And Damayanti has some mysteries to solve.&nbsp; Appa has&nbsp; reported that his checked handkerchief is missing.&nbsp; Paati has reported that her hairpin is missing.

One of Damayanti’s favourite socks is also&nbsp; missing. She bites into her Specially Sticky Chikki.&nbsp; Now who would steal just one sock? Like any detective worth her snacks, Damayanti has a list of suspects.

Suspect No. 1 : Sillie Millie Suspect No. 2 : Pushpa Flowerpot Suspect No. 3 : Moody Mani

Sillie Millie is her brother’s best friend.

Millie is always giggling, and wears&nbsp; mismatched bands and socks ALL&nbsp; the time. But why in the sky would&nbsp; Millie want Appa’s hankie?

Pushpa Flowerpot is Appa’s&nbsp; music teacher.

Pushpa Flowerpot could hide anything in&nbsp; her hair. It is like a big nest. But why in the&nbsp; sky would she stuff a sock in there?

Moody Mani lives with Upstairs Uncle.

Moody Mani loves fresh newspaper and fresh laundry.&nbsp; But why in the sky would Mani want Paati’s hairpin?

Snacking is Damayanti’s superpower but&nbsp; sometimes a girl needs to think on her feet. She&nbsp; takes her dabba and goes for a walk. A detective&nbsp; must keep her eyes and ears wide open.

Damayanti looks left , right ,

down , up ...

Damayanti sees a necklace hanging from&nbsp; a branch of the mango tree.&nbsp; She hears a high-pitched squeak.

Now what in the tree is that?

All this time, she has been thinking about the WRONG nest.

Damayanti has found the thief!

A baby squirrel sitting inside her sock! Appa’s old hankie is lining the nest. Paati’s hairpin is in there, too. A beady necklace, a shiny hairpin... tiny treasures for a tiny baby.

The baby squirrel looks so cosy that Damayanti decides to leave her other sock behind. Just in&nbsp; case the mother wants to add it to the nest.

One less sock is not a bad thing.&nbsp; But one&nbsp; more snack?&nbsp; Always a good thing.
