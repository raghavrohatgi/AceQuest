# My Home Holds Us All

My home is a pile of boxes and so many doors and windows. A door belongs to our family, too. But my home is spread all around. It is so much larger than our little flat.

My home is bright, sunny,

dark and damp.

and sometimes wet.

It is yellow, red, pink

and all kinds of green.

My home is quiet sometimes,

but mostly loud. It barks, chimes and sings, too!

Can you hear Kaalu the dog, Pakya and his cawing lot, Maaya and her kittens?

Aaji’s pickles, a whole chatter of squirrels and the dusty afternoon sun — my home holds us all.

My home is

Asmi, Falak, Roshan, Samay, Jhilmil. And so many doors &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;and windows.

My home is perfect for hide-and-seek!
