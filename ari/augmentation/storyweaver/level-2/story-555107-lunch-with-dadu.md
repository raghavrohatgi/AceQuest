# Lunch with Dadu

Every Saturday afternoon, we have lunch at Dadu’s house.

On hot days, he makes us&nbsp; a big bowl of curd rice.

But what we really like to eat are the pickles, podi, chutneys and chips.

Dadu always eats the karela pickle&nbsp; first. It is so bitter!

We like the aam chhunda. It is sticky and sweet.

Timtim wants more amla chutney. It is so sour!

And who finished all the potato chips? Minu, of course — he loves salty food.

My favourite is the chutney podi. It is spicy.

I think I will just eat plain curd rice!
