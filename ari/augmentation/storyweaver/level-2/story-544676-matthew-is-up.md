# Matthew is up!

The sun is up. Matthew is up.

Mommy is sleeping.&nbsp; Daddy is sleeping.&nbsp; Lizwi is sleeping.

Matthew is hungry.&nbsp; Matthew&nbsp; pours the milk on his cereal.&nbsp; Matthew eats.&nbsp; Matthew eats&nbsp; it all up.&nbsp; Mommy is sleeping. Daddy is sleeping. Lizwi is sleeping.

Matthew needs to poo. He sits on the loo and wipes his own bottom. Mommy is sleeping. Daddy is sleeping. Lizwi is sleeping.

Matthew pulls off his pyjamas. He puts on his clothes. First he puts on his Spiderman underpants.&nbsp; Then he puts on his number 1 shirt.&nbsp; Then he puts on his pants.

Mommy is sleeping. Daddy is sleeping. Lizwi is sleeping.

So Matthew jumps. Matthew jumps small jumps in his room.

Mommy is still sleeping. Daddy is still sleeping. Lizwi is still sleeping.

So Matthew jumps. Matthew jumps in the&nbsp;passage. Matthew&nbsp;jumps in&nbsp; the bathroom.

Mommy is still sleeping. Daddy is still sleeping. Lizwi is still sleeping.

So Matthew jumps on them. Matthew jumps&nbsp; on Mommy,&nbsp; on Daddy,&nbsp; on Lizwi,&nbsp; on the big bed.

Lizwi wakes up.&nbsp; Lizwi jumps! Matthew and Lizwi jump on Mommy and Daddy! Mommy wakes up.
