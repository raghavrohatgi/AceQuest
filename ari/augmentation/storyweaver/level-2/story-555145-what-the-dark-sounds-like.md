# What the Dark Sounds Like

I wanted to know what the dark sounds like So I listened to a night.

One dog barking and a lot of quiet.

I wanted to know what wonder feels like

So I touched a tree.

Rough and strong, and perfect for hugging.

I wanted to know what love smells like —

that one was easy.

Cake! (And the ones who bake it.)

I wanted to know what confusion tastes like

So I ate a dream. Crunchy and tangy, sweet and chewy — I can almost&nbsp; remember it.

I wanted to know what magic looks like So I looked up at the clouds

and down at the mud.

Magic was all around me.
