# Nelson the Magician

Nelson the Magician’s family has many magicians.&nbsp; His Aunt Mariyam’s Marvellous Missing Mango trick&nbsp; is world-famous for being marvellous. His Great Grandfather Cyril Jr is always in the news. Even at the age of 103.&nbsp; And his (annoying) Cousin Juby is constantly winning awards.

Sometimes Nelson wonders: &nbsp; “What kind of magician will I be?”

Today is Nelson’s very first magic show ever.&nbsp; He arrives at Zora’s birthday party. All the children are excited. “Make Zo’s cat disappear!” “And then make Zo disappear!” “What’s under your cape?” “I don’t like magic.” &nbsp; Almost all of the children are excited. &nbsp;

“For my first trick, I will make this ball disappear,” Nelson announces. “Jeem Boooom Gili Gili Baaaa! Make this ball go very, very faaaaaar!”

“Next, I shall place this egg under my armpit and magically move it to my bag of wonders. See? The bag is completely empty!”

Anda, muttai, how do you doooo?” “Chhu montor chhuuuuu!

“Moving on! Watch as my shiny magician’s hat rises up and floats in the air.” Nelson taps his hat three times, smartly, with his wand, and chants, “Ikdam pikdam palam po, tedha topi up you go!”

“Never mind! For my final trick, I will remove this table cloth without disturbing a single item on top of it. “Ajji Majji La Tarajji Ajji Majji Mej Tarajji!”

Zora’s mother is not happy.

Nelson is sad. He now knows what kind of a magician he is. A very, very, very… CLUMSY one.&nbsp;Nobody wants a clumsy magician. They want marvellous magicians.&nbsp; Famous magicians. Award-winning magicians.

The next day, Nelson gets some phone calls.&nbsp; Someone wants him to perform at the Christmas mela!&nbsp; Next, the local hospital asks if he can come cheer up the children. He is even invited to put on a show at the public library. Nelson is confused. After all, he is a clumsy magician. And nobody wants one of those—right?

Wrong. It turns out that all the children LOVE Nelson.

“Make that hat catch on fire!” &nbsp; “Can you do the anda muttai trick?” “What’s in your bag of wonders today?” “I don’t like magic!” Well, almost all of them.

Nelson&nbsp; The Amazingly Clumsy Magician Call for birthdays, weddings, melas and more!
