# Baba in a Sari

This afternoon, Ma is as busy as a bee, It’s playtime for Baba and me.

Abracadabra!&nbsp; There is magic in the air, As Ma’s saris flutter everywhere.

On colourful saris, dance gold and silver vines, A hide-and-seek of dots and lines.

Baba is my model for the day.

I drape saris on him, th is way and that way.

Look, look, look, my beautiful Baba, Royal, like a maharaja.

Our fashion show is a hit. And Baba in a sari? Superhit!
