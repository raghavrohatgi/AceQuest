# Chupuk Chapaak

“Where are your shoes, Konkon?” asked Mithinga.

“Don’t like them. Don’t need them. How else would I do this?” Shrip Shrup Shrap!

“Or this!” Oimili joined in. The pebbles tickled her feet.

Ahhh! The sand was soft and warm. “Look!” said Mithinga, pointing towards the stream.

“Good morning, Bini Baideo!” “Hello children! I was just listening to the kulu-kulu-kulu of the stream.” “Did you also hear the swooo-swooo-swooo of the wind?” asked Konkon.

“Can class be outdoors today?” asked Oimili. “Please?” Konkon joined in.

Bini Baideo smiled.
