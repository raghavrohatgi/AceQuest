# I Can Smell...

What is it that I smell?

Mm m m …

Is it the fresh milk that Hari Kaka is carrying?

Is it the elaichi that Dida is having with her paan?

Or is it the palm jaggery that Suku Da is making?

I think it is the fine rice that Abu Jetha is selling.

What is it that I smell?

And where is it coming from?

Ohhh! It is kheer! Ma is making kheer.
