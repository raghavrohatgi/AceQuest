# Sonth Is Here

I always love winter and snow.

Winter is a great umbrella&nbsp; to not see anyone.

This is the time to cosy up with a kanger.

To catch snowflakes on my tongue.&nbsp; To eat icicles and build snow monsters.

Blossom by blossom, spring arrives. Wildflowers bloom first.

Then the mustard fields and almond trees. The smell of the warm earth blooms with sudden and beautiful colours. I tumble out of my den and forget the winter that I love.

Birds take flight into the blue sky. The visiting mallards join the hoopoes and whistling thrushes&nbsp; to sing melodious tunes. Birds build nests and feed their younglings.

Rivers and streams gush with fresh water.&nbsp; I can’t stop watching the grass dance to its flow.

It’s time for picnics.&nbsp; I love the warmth of the sun...&nbsp; though the wind is chilly.&nbsp; I walk in the fields to see flowers scatter their smiles everywhere.

For days now shepherds will be on the move. They’ll take sheep from the plains to graze in the alpine meadows.

Schools will finally open after the long winter break.&nbsp; I really miss Daisy, Lilly, Abdul, Hina and Rosy.&nbsp; I can’t wait to see them again. Sonth is here.
