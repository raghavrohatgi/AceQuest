# We Have Grown so Much!

Ankita, Ritu, Rohan, and Sunil are sitting in class. Asma Madam is announcing the exam results.&nbsp; Ankita takes the papers from Madam.&nbsp; She is scared to check her marks but she tells herself, ‘It’s okay I will not make the same mistakes next time.’&nbsp;&nbsp; ‘Yay yay yayy I passed!’ she happily tells Ritu.

Asma Madam calls the students who are selected for Sports Day. Rohan really wants to play for the school team. He tells himself ‘I tried my best, it is okay even if I am not selected.’ Then Asma Madam calls his name and Sunil’s name. ‘Hurray hurray hurray!’ says Rohan feeling excited. Asma Madam calls the students who are selected for Sports Day.&nbsp; Rohan really wants to play for the school team.&nbsp; He tells himself ‘I tried my best, it is okay even if I am not selected.’&nbsp; Then Asma Madam calls his name and Sunil’s name.&nbsp; ‘Hurray hurray hurray!’ says Rohan feeling excited.

Harsh rushes to Ritu.&nbsp; Trr trr trr ‘Harsh Bhaiya, why are you so excited?’ asks Ritu. ‘Ahaha, I will be helping our school students for the Art for Change competition in Almora,’ says Harsh excitedly. ‘Wow that is great!’ says Ritu.

Ritu is proud of her brother. When Harsh Bhaiya first started painting he would drop colours everywhere. He would not finish any paintings, but start a new one everyday. Now Harsh Bhaiya knows what he likes to paint and enjoys painting more.

Anku is happy that she passed but she wants to study harder for her next exam.&nbsp; She tells her mother about her plan.&nbsp; ‘Mummy, I have decided to study every day from 3-5 in the afternoon,’ says Anku. ‘That’s a good plan Anku.&nbsp; Remember Anku you can always ask for help,’ tells her mother.

‘Sunil, will you practice with me everyday?’ asks Rohan. ‘Yes Rohan, we are good but we can do better.&nbsp; We need to practice to win,’ says Sunil. ‘Okay then I will meet you at 6 o’clock tomorrow morning outside your house,’ says Rohan.

In the next month, Anku and Rohan both work very hard. Anku studies for two hours. Sometimes when she does not understand something, she asks Mummy, Ritu, and Asma Madam to help her. Anku now knows a lot about India’s history.&nbsp; After studying, Anku practices different hairstyles on Ritu.&nbsp; Now Anku knows how hairstyles can look very different on different types of&nbsp; hair.

Everyone hears the Thak thak thak of Rohan’s bat before the Coo coo coo of the hens.&nbsp; Sometimes when he does not want&nbsp; to wake up,&nbsp; Sunil knocks at his door and pulls him out of bed. Knock knock knock knock Practicing everyday has helped Rohan. He does not get tired even after making 50 runs. He is sure that they will win the match. Anku and Rohan learnt so much by practicing.&nbsp; What steps&nbsp; have you taken to grow?
