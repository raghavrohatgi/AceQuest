# Aalepak for Tatya

Shakil Tatya drove here in his purple Nano. It is always fun when he visits us.

That night, Tatya and Baba cooked dinner together.&nbsp;Aai got a break. She and I watched a movie. After dinner, the three of them sat&nbsp; and talked. Aai and Baba enjoy&nbsp; listening to Tatya.

At dawn, Baba left to milk the buffaloes.&nbsp; Tatya woke up late. We went with Aai to see the calf. Seeing the vest Baba was wearing, Tatya said,“I want that!” “No need to take mine,” Baba said. “We will buy you a new one.”

We all went to the town market to buy a new vest for Tatya. I sat in the front seat of his car and played songs.

The market was crowded and full&nbsp; of colours.&nbsp; Baba led the way towards a clothes shop. What a grand time I was having.

At the shop, we looked at different vests for Tatya.&nbsp; But Tatya couldn’t fit into any of them! “Show us a bigger one, please,” Baba asked the shopkeeper.

Tatya fit into the bigger vest. He bought four of them.

Then he saw some striped underpants. “Give me those, too,” Tatya said. He spotted some thick blankets. “I’ll take those, too,” Tatya said.

When we left home, Tatya was supposed to buy one vest. But once we reached the market, he bought so many more things! Would such a tiny Nano have room for all the things&nbsp; he bought?

Phew! Tatya managed to fit everything in his car.

Just then, a man passed by shouting, “Aalepak!” “What is aalepak?” I asked Baba. “It is ginger barfi,” &nbsp; Baba said.&nbsp; “It is&nbsp; made &nbsp; &nbsp;with ginger,&nbsp; sugar &nbsp; &nbsp; and cardamom.” “It is delicious,” Tatya said. “Do you want some?” Aai asked me.&nbsp; And she put a ten-r upee note in my pocket. paak le Aaa

I ran towards the aalepak-seller. “How much is it for?” “Five rupees!” “Give me one, please,” I said.

Walking back to Aai, I read the words written on the packet: ‘Great for journeys’. So I ran back to the aalepak-seller. I bought another piece of aalepak with the rest of the money.

Tatya was waiting for me. I gave him one piece of aalepak and said,&nbsp; “Tatya, have a great journey back!”

Tatya was delighted. “Thank you, Chingi. There is just enough room&nbsp; in my Nano for this aalepak.” And with the piece of aalepak, Shakil Tatya started his great journey back home.
