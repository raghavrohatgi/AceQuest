# Exam Fever

Just four days to go, The exams were close, When Ma’s fever Rose and rose!

Dadi made a soothing drink, She sang a calming melody. But the exam monster Just would not let us be.

Papa laid a wet cloth On Ma’s burning brow.

He combed her hair, Fed her khichdi somehow, Because…

While notes and studies filled the days,&nbsp; Fast and frantic, forever more, Ma’s fever Did soar and roar!

Bhaiya read chapters From a textbook, out loud. He talked about standing first, “Ma, won’t that make you proud?”

“Don’t be scared,” I said, Prancing like a clown. Telling funny tales till A smile replaced Ma’s frown. Because…

No one can visit— Neither neighbour nor friend, When Ma’s fever Burns without end.

Finally the exam day came along. Ma woke up well and strong No more fever, no more worry.

And you know what?

My Ma got her BA degree!
