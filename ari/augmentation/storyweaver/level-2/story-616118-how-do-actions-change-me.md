# How Do Actions Change Me?

Rohan loves to cook. Today he is cooking Rajma. He packs an extra tiffin for his friend Sunil.&nbsp; After school, Rohan and Sunil eat rajma chawal .&nbsp;&nbsp; " Mmm mmm mmm, Rohan, this is so yummy. I feel so happy! "&nbsp; says Sunil.&nbsp; &nbsp; " Hehehe… Sunil, you have some chawal on your face, " says Rohan.

Veer, comes to the ground. " Parnam Veer, try some rajma , Rohan made it, " says Sunil. " Hahaha... no I don’t want anything from this little boy who cooks like girls, " shouts Veer. Veer always makes fun of his height and his cooking. Rohan feels sad and leaves the ground.

" Rohan are you okay? " asks Sunil. " I swim and cycle but my height does not grow. " says Rohan. " Rohan you don’t have to change how you look. Veer has to change his thoughts. " " Why can’t boys cook? " " Rohan boys can cook.&nbsp; Everyone from school loves Ramu&nbsp; chacha’s chow mein.&nbsp; Don’t listen to Veer, " says Sunil.

﻿" You’re right. I like how I look and everyone can cook. Veer can change his thoughts, ﻿" says Rohan. ﻿" Mmm mmm mmm, now let me finish your yummy rajma chawal ﻿." ﻿ ﻿" Hahaha… Sunil, you are the best, ﻿" &nbsp;says Rohan happily.

Rohan and Sunil are going back home when they see Ankita, Ritu, and Soni.&nbsp;&nbsp;Ankita is trying new hairstyles on Ritu and Soni.&nbsp; Ritu’s hair is long Anku makes two beautiful plaits out of them. " I love these long plaits I am excited to show this to everyone! " says Ritu.

Ankita has never made a hairstyle on curly hair. She is excited to try a new puff style on Soni’s hair. " Wow! This looks wonderful! " says Soni looking in the mirror. Soni wants to show this hairstyle to everyone, but as soon as she gets up, the puff falls. " Oh no Anku! Look the clip&nbsp; came off, " says Soni. "Let me try again, " says Anku. ﻿" Anku I want to play, I cannot&nbsp;play if the puff falls.&nbsp; Can you try&nbsp;this next time? ﻿" asks Soni. ﻿" Okay Soni, ﻿" says Anku sadly. Soni goes out to play.

" Why could I not make the puff hairstyle? How will I become a hairstylist now? " says Anku. " No Anku. Look, you made my hair look great! " says Ritu. " But it did not work on Soni’s hair. I should be able to style everyone's hair. &nbsp;Maybe I should not be a hairstylist, "&nbsp; says Anku. " Anku, this is the first time you tried a hairstyle on curly hair, right? " asks Sunil. " Yes, it is the first time. I feel so sad. I don’t want to give up my dream, " says Anku. " You don’t have to give up your dream, " says Rohan. " What should I do? " asks Anku, feeling confused.

" My aunty is a hairstylist in Rishikesh she can help you, " says Ritu. " Yes, and you can try again tomorrow, " says Sunil. " You think my dream will come true? " asks Anku. " Yes, it will we all are here to help you, "&nbsp; says Rohan. " Thank you, everyone, I feel very loved. I will ask Ritu’s aunt and try again tomorrow, " Anku.
