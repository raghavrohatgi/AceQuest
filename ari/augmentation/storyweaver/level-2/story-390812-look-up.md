# Look Up!

Wow, look up! What do you see?

In the morning .. Small sparrows and big hadedas.

In the daytime... A flutter of butterflies over flowers.

A sugarbird with its long tail. A sunbird with its bright red collar.

A yellow-billed kite soaring high in the sky.

In the late afternoon... Bees buzzing to their hive.

Geese flying home. As the sun sets ...

The twinkling lights of an aeroplane full of people.

Bats eating insects for their dinner.

When it’s nice and dark ... An owl swooping over a field.

A shooting star.

Even when we’re dreaming there is so much to see!
