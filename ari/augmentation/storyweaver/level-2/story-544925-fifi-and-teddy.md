# Fifi and Teddy

Fifi has loved Teddy&nbsp; since she was a baby.

Fifi eats with Teddy. Fifi plays with Teddy.

Fifi takes Teddy wherever she goes.

And they curl up together every night.

Fifi knows she cannot take Teddy to school.&nbsp; So every morning she leaves Teddy on her bed.

One day, when she comes back from school, Teddy is not there!

“What’s wrong, Fifi?” asks Mom.

Teddy is missing!

“No, darling. Teddy just had a bath!”

“There you are, Teddy!”
