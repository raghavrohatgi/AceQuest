# How Big Is a Whale Shark?

How long is a whale shark? Longer than a giant grouper But shorter than a siphonophore.

How heavy is a whale shark? Heavier than 100 common dolphins.

But lighter than a humpback whale.

How deep can a whale shark dive?

Down to 2,000 metres, but not as deep as the Arabian Sea.

How fast can a whale shark swim? Faster than Greenland sharks.

But slower than a swordfish.

How big is a whale shark? Smaller than a blue whale.

But bigger than any other fish in the sea.
