# Who Is Incharge of the Payment?

It is raining heavily in the village. Lalu Chacha takes his truck out. He reaches the road near the river. Suddenly his truck stops. Lalu Chacha steps out but his leg gets pulled in the daldal. ‘Oh no, not again!’ he shouts. Every year this road becomes a daldal*. There is no other road to reach other villages. Lalu Chacha shouts for help. Some people help him get out of the daldal.&nbsp; *Daldal: Marsh

﻿ In the evening Chacha visits ward member Rajesh. He tells him about the incident. Rajesh says he will try to convince the Gram Panchayat to solve this problem. Entire monsoon season passes without any progress. The villagers lose hope but one day the Gram Panchayat* announces a road project passed by Zila Panchayat*. The construction will be done under MGNREGA work. The villagers are overjoyed. They get employment and a pakka* road. *Gram Panchayat: Local governance body in a village *Zila Panchayat: Electoral governance body of a district *Pakka road: Concrete road

The work for the road starts.&nbsp; Lalu Chacha and other villagers work for seven hours everyday.&nbsp; In a few months the road is complete but villagers have not received any money.

﻿ ﻿ All the villagers go to the Thekedar* to ask for their salaries. ‘Don’t worry you will get your money. I am not in charge of this, go ask your ward member,’ he says. The next day they go to the ward member Rajesh. ‘Don’t worry you will get your money. I am not in charge of this go ask the Gram Pradhan* ,’ he says. *Thekedar: Contractor&nbsp; *Gram Pradhan: Electoral head of the local governance body of a village

The next day they go to Gram Pradhan. ‘Don’t worry you will get your money. Zila Panchayat* told us they will inform us as soon as they pass the money,’ he says. The villagers are upset and angry. Nobody is taking responsibility for the payment. Months go by without any answer. *Zila Panchayat: Electoral governance body of a district

Finally, one day the Panchayat announces that the money will be transferred in their bank accounts by Friday. On Friday everyone goes to the bank. They take out the cash from their Bank accounts.&nbsp;When it is Lalu Chacha’s turn, the accountant says his account does not have money.&nbsp; ‘How can this happen?’ Lalu Chacha is shocked.

He goes to talk to the bank manager.&nbsp; Next in line is Charu Chachi. The accountant says she has twenty thousand rupees.&nbsp; Chachi is surprised. She also goes to the bank manager.&nbsp; ‘First you don’t pay us on time and when the payments are done, there are mistakes. I want my money today,’ demands Lalu Chacha.&nbsp; When Charu Chachi listens to this she understands what has happened.

‘I have received ten thousand extra and Lalu Chacha has not received any payment,’ says Charu Chachi to both of them.&nbsp; ‘Oh no, that means my money has gone in Chachi’s account,’ says Lalu Chacha. The manager apologizes for this mistake. The problem is solved in another hour. After six months Lalu Chacha finally receives his salary.&nbsp; He goes home happy but tired.
