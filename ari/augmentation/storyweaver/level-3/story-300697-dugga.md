# Dugga

Meet the real life Dugga This book is based on the real-life story of Dugga the dog. She was found severely injured on a street in Bangalore. She was rescued and rehabilitated. Dugga now lives with her pack of two cats and two humans.&nbsp; Dugga thanks the doctors and staff at the veterinary hospital where she was treated. Her best bum-wags and face-licks are reserved for the surgeon who repaired her badly damaged urinary and digestive system, amputated her shattered hind leg, and guided her on her long and difficult path to recovery. Dugga is also grateful to the many people who came forward to help with their time, money and kindness.&nbsp; The city can be a dangerous place for the animals that live in it. Animals are notoriously bad at crossing roads, and automobiles almost always seem like they are in a tearing hurry. If you come across an injured animal that you can’t help by yourself, perhaps you can help find someone who can. If you are in a vehicle that is being driven rashly, politely ask the person at the wheel to slow down. And please, save your brightest smile for the person in your own neighbourhood who takes care of street animals.
