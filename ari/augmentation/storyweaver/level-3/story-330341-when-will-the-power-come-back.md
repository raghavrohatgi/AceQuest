# When Will the Power Come Back?

Chutki and Pinku are attending online classes at Chutki’s house. In today’s class we are learning about the Anganwadi* center. Teacher requests Chutki to read. Chutki reads “Rita is the….” and suddenly the screen is black. The light goes out, the battery dies and the phone switches off. Sometimes the powercut lasts for days. They were worried and angry.&nbsp; *Anganwadi: Rural child care center for children between age 3 and 6 &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;

The children come out of the house. They meet Sonam and Anuj there.&nbsp; ‘Oh no! I’m tired of these power cuts,’ said Pinku.&nbsp; ‘When will the power come back? When will we study?’ said Sonam. ‘Remember when we went to Gram Sabha?’ asked Anuj.&nbsp; ‘Yes, the problem of water was solved there,’ said Chutki. ‘Maybe we can talk about this problem in the Gram Sabha*,’ said Anuj.&nbsp; ‘That’s a great idea. We should talk to Salim bhai, the electrician. He will know what to do,’ said Pinku. *Gram Sabha: Gram Sabha is the meeting organised by the local elected governance body with people in a village.

They all go to Salim Bhai’s shop. He is fixing Chaman Chacha’s fan. The children tell him about the problem.&nbsp; ‘I cannot help you with this, children,’ says Salim Bhai.&nbsp; ‘There has to be some solution. We cannot study like this,’ says Chutki.&nbsp; ‘In Kimchi they have put up a solar power plant. If we had one then we would have electricity all the time,’ says Chaman Chacha.

‘Why can’t we have one?’ they asked. ‘Everyone from the village needs to agree,’ he replied. ‘Everyone in the village faces problems because of electricity, I think they will agree,’ said Sonam. ‘Yes, we can fix this like we fixed the water shortage problem,’ said Salim bhai. The children are happy to see Salim Bhai encouraged.

The next day Salim bhai spoke with other villagers. Lalu Chacha and Krishna Bua agreed to help. Villagers had doubts about the money. Salim bhai has a solution for this. He suggests that the Gram Panchayat* can fund this project. After a week, more and more villagers are convinced about the Solar Power project. They decide to discuss this idea in the Gram Sabha.&nbsp; *Gram Panchayat: Local governance body in a village

﻿ The Sarpanch* has doubts about the budget and the availability of land. Krishna Bua suggests using the roofs of the school to set up this plant. The villagers convinced the Sarpanch to request for Vidhayak* funds to fund the project. The Sarpanch had no choice when everyone wanted this. She agrees and connects with the Vidhayak . The villagers and children are overjoyed. They know they can fix any problem together. *Sarpanch: Electoral head of the local governance body of a village *Vidhayak: Member of legislative assembly
