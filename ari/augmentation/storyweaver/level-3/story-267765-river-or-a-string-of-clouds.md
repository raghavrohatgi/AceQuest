# River or a string of clouds?

The river is a cloud’s piggy bank,

Where it stores&nbsp; the small change of drops, Is it a river, tell me,&nbsp; or a string of clouds?

The river is a flowing city, For so many lives it’s their home, pretty!

The river is where our thirst lives, To the fish, their precious breath it gives.

The river is an alligator’s daily clock,&nbsp; Is it a river or a string of clouds?

The river brightens up the faces of fields, For clouds, the farmer's eyes are always peeled.

The river changes the colour of soils, From tree to tree the river coils.

Look, by the tree on the mounds!&nbsp; Is it a river or a string of clouds?

He catches water droplets one by one, The clever fisherman Sun.

The river will be completely new, Whenever you look at it again.

In its hand the river holds a magic wand, Is it a river or a string of clouds?
