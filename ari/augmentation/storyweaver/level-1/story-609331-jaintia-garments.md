# Jaiñtia Garments

Muka and Blouse Paila Merina Khyrwa ŋ

Yuspoŋ Ryndia Tlem Butoi Paila Yuslieñ

'Jaiñtia Garments,' is one of the three books which is part of 'The Garment Series.' 'The Garment Series,' &nbsp;is part of the 45 Meghalaya-contextual storybooks curated under The Forgotten Folklore Project (TFFP), an initiative of the Sauramandala Foundation. The TFFP team is grateful to Ms. Rully Ryngkhlem for sharing extensively on the different types of Jaiñtia garments used by the people of the Jaiñtia community, without which this book would not have been possible.
