# The Blue Fruit

Kasni looked at the tree carefully. There were blue fruits on the green tree.

She found this strange.

She ran and went to her mother...

and said, “Amma, there are blue fruits on green trees.” Her mother was also surprised.

She came outside with Kasni.

Kasni pointed at the tree and said, “Look there!” Her mother looked at the tree and laughed.

Oh dear, that is not a fruit, that is a kite! A blue kite was tangled around the green leaves of the tree.

Kasni now started to laugh along with her mother.
