# A Scary Pit

Samira was riding her cycle.

Suddenly, she heard the sound of a goat. Samira turned to look…

a goat was looking into a pit and bleating.

Samira quickly got off her cycle. She crouched down to look into the pit.

A baby goat had fallen into the pit. She was trapped.

Samira bravely climbed down into the pit.

She then picked up the baby goat and got her out of the pit…

and the baby goat happily hopped along and was with her mother once again.
