# Mum Said

Mom says Va can crawl.

Mom says Ngou can walk.

Mom says Soma can dance Chai Yam. * *Chai Yam: a Khmer dance

Mom says Vari can sew a pocket onto a pair of pants.

Mom says Nara can play the chapei. * *Chapei: a Khmer musical instrument

Mom finds out Grandpa Ro is sick.

Mom brings Va, Ngou, Soma, Vari, and Nara to Grandpa Ro’s house.

Grandpa Ro sees what his grandchildren can do.&nbsp; Grandpa Ro praises his grandchildren.&nbsp; Grandpa is not sick anymore.
