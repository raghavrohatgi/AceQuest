# So Many Guests

Ma, look. We have two guests. Sister Sparrow and Cousin Mynah.

Here is Aunt Koel. Now we have three guests.

Come in, Brother Crow. Now we have four.

Here comes Sister Pigeon. That makes it five guests.

This way...come slowly, Aunt Ducky. So now we have six guests.

Brother Peacock, do come in. Now we have seven guests.

Ah! Come in and sit, Grandpa Parrot. That makes it eight guests.

Welcome, Granny Owl. Now we have nine guests.

Come right in, Uncle Stork. But why are you standing?

Hmm, so it all adds up to ten guests. Wait a moment. How did you all know…

…that it is my birthday today? Ma, you told them, did you not?
