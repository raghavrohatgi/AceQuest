# The Fountain

“You have had a nice and clean bath. Now play right here.”

“And don’t you play in the mud.”

“Look, a fountain!”

“Don’t! My clothes will get wet.”

“But it’s my fountain. I made it. Only I can play with it.”

“So much water wasted! Don’t you kids have school today?”

“Look at you, Ramesh…What a mess. I just gave you a bath!”
