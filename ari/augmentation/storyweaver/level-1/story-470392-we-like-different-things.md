# We like Different Things

“Hi! I am Yangjee.”

“Hey! Come here,” Yangjee calls to the girl. ”What is your name?” The girl says, “Hi! My name is Lucky.”

”Yangjee,” Lucky says, ”why are you so small?” “I am not small,” Yangjee replies. ”You are just tall!”

Yangjee says, “Play ball with me, Lucky!” “Okay, okay, I am coming,” Lucky says.

“I like to draw with you,” Lucky says. “I like to play dolls with you,” Yangjee says.

“I love crackers. You like momos , * ” Lucky says. “I like to play chungi, * ” Yangjee says.&nbsp; ”You like to jump rope.” *Momos are dumplings common to Nepal, Tibet, and nearby countries *Chungi is a game similar to Hacky Sack played with a ball of rubber bands. &nbsp;It is played in Nepal and Northeastern India.

“We like different things,” Lucky says. ”But we are still best friends, ” Yangjee says.

From a distance they hear Yangjee’s mom yell, ”Come and have your food,” in the Tamang language.

From a distance they hear Lucky’s mom yell, ”Come and have your food,” in the Nepal Bhasa language.

“Let us go eat,” Yangjee says.
