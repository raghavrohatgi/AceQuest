# I Don't Know

Appu said, “Aai, I’m feeling good.” “Why?” asked Aai. “I don’t know.”

“Shh! Quiet, the baby will wake up.”

“Why are you angry?” Aai asked. “I don’t know,” replied Appu.

“When you’re very hungry, you get angry, don't you?” said Aai.

“I am bored,” grumbled Appu.

“Come, let’s play,” said Baba.

“Aaji, Aaji, I’m afraid,” said Appu. “What are you afraid of, my dear?” asked Aaji. “I don’t know.”

“Feeling better now?” she asked. Just then, Aai called.

“Appu, come and see this!”

“How lovely the night looks!”

“Your fear has gone away, I think! Did the fireflies take it?” Aai asked. Appu giggled. “I don’t know.”
