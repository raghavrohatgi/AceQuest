# Aaji's Terrace

We love going to our Aaji's house!

The big terrace is our favourite spot.

Diwali looks so colourful from the terrace.

During our Christmas holidays, we fly kites.

We play there under the summer sun.

Our dolls love the terrace too.

We sleep under the stars.

Aaji dries her papad on the terrace. We shoo away the birds that try to steal them.

I can pluck a guava from the nearby tree.

I once saw a monkey on the guava tree. I ran away.

Aaji laughed and said, “Oh, he is here to meet his friends from Mumbai.”
