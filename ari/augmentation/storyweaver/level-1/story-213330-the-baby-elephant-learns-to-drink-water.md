# The Baby Elephant Learns To Drink Water

A baby elephant was walking in the jungle. It felt thirsty while walking.

It reached a river alone. After reaching there, it thought, how can I drink the water?

First, it dipped its ears in the river’s water. But it could not drink the water.

Then, it dipped its tail in the water. Even then, it could not drink the water.

Then its mother reached there...

the mother slowly came towards the baby elephant.

The mother dipped its baby’s trunk in the water...

and the baby elephant started to drink the water!
