# Garo Garments

Do·me Kabong Nadongari Ripok do·katchi Seng·ki Re·king

Kotip Sisa Pandra Gando Ripok

'Garo Garments,' is one of the three books which is part of 'The Garment Series.' 'The Garment Series,' &nbsp;is part of the 45 Meghalaya-contextual storybooks curated under The Forgotten Folklore Project (TFFP), an initiative of the Sauramandala Foundation. The TFFP team is grateful to Arak M Sangma for sharing extensively on the different types of Garo garments used by the Garo community, without which this book would not have been possible.
