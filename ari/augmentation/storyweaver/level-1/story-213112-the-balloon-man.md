# The Balloon Man

Here comes the balloon man. He has so many colourful balloons.

Chiku comes running towards him. She happily takes a yellow balloon.

Miku, Bhiku, Binu call out for him. Oh balloon man, wait! Wait!

Just then a strong wind blows. Chiku runs away with her balloon.
