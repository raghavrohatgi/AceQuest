# Urmila's Umbrella

Urmila picked up a piece of paper.

She sharpened a pencil…

and drew an umbrella on the paper.

While colouring in the beautiful umbrella, she tore the paper.

Urmila decided to get an old newspaper…

She tore it and made a newspaper umbrella, but she wasn’t happy with it…

She was feeling hungry. She took an orange from the kitchen. While peeling the orange, she realised that its peel is just like an umbrella!

With excitement, Urmila stuck the peel on top of the pencil. Urmila’s Umbrella was ready!
