# Pakhi the Pigeon

Pakhi had a dream one night. In the dream, she was a crow.

She flew high onto the roof of someone’s house. She was hungry…

and started to caw loudly! A girl heard her and appeared.

She threw some seeds near Pakhi. Pakhi started gobbling them up!

But ouch! She felt a small stone in her mouth. She started to shriek.

From her own shriek, Pakhi suddenly woke up. Her mother asked her – what happened? Why did you shriek?

Pakhi wobbled all her teeth to see if they were fine.

She then said to her mother that she was just having a dream. It was time to go back to sleep…
