# Zera and Jojo

It was Zera’s birthday. He wanted to invite Jojo, but his mother said no!

Both Zera and Jojo felt very sad.

The next day when Jojo came to the party, Zera smiled and became happy.

Jojo told Zera that Zera's mother invited him to the party.

JoJo carried Zera on his back.

The party was over and it was time to say goodbye. As Jojo walked away, Zera and his mother started to cry.
