You only have to work as a team,’ says Asma Madam. ‘What do you want to make?’ asks Rohan. Our School What do you want to make? &nbsp; An&nbsp; airplane &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp; A&nbsp; Flower

‘I want to make a big flower,’ says Ritu.&nbsp; ‘But I want to draw our school,’ says Anku.&nbsp; ‘And I want to make an airplane. What will we do now?’ asks Rohan. &nbsp; I listen to all my friends and makes sure they are comfortable to share their ideas. ‘Let us make everything!’ says&nbsp; Sunil. ‘Great idea! Sunil always finds a way to accept everyone’s ideas,’ says Rohan.

Everyone removes their brushes and colours except Anku.&nbsp; She gave them to Soni. They share a box of colours. Anku feels shy to ask for colours from others. ‘Here Anku, take this.
