# Posto, Please Clean Your Desk!

“Posto, please clean your desk!” Ma says.

﻿ I bunch up the ten pencils. 10

I clean the ten paintbrushes. 10 + 10 = 20

I put the ten crayons back into the box. 10 + 10 + 10 = 30

I cap the ten paint bottles. 10 + 10 + 10 + 10 = 40

I arrange the ten books. 10 + 10 + 10 + 10 + 10 = 50

“Good job, Posto!” says Ma.
