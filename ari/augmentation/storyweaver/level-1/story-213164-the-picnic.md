# The Picnic

There was a dense jungle.

A group of friends came there…

and had a lot of fun!

Together they played rat–a–tat...

and got hungry after that. They cut up apples, cabbage, carrots and tomatoes and made a nice salad.

Everyone enjoyed the salad together.
