# At the River

Ma, Dada, and Akash went to the river.

There were many people there.

Ma began washing clothes.

Splash! Dada jumped into the river.

Akash could not see Dada in the river!

Akash was afraid. He shouted, “Dada…! Dada…!”

Dada quickly came up from under the water.

“Aku, come in for a ride!”

Ma put Akash on Dada’s shoulders.

They both played in the water. &nbsp;Akash splashed the water. Splish-Splosh!

It was so much fun!
