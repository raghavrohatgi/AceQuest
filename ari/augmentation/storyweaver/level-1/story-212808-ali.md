# Ali

Ali was studying at home.

Suddenly he noticed the tap outside the door.

Water from the tap was dripping on the ground.

Ali put a pot under the tap.

The water now started dripping into the pot.

Ali started studying again. After a while, the pot became filled with water.

Ali poured water from the pot in the plants.
