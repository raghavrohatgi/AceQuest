# What's That Sound?

The clock struck midnight. Reena was lying wide awake in her bed.

She was not able to sleep. Suddenly, she heard a strange sound.

She felt very scared by the sound. She picked up the stick in her room…

and quietly stepped outside.

The noise was coming from near her father’s bed…

she slowly moved toward the bed. She thought that the sound was coming from under the bed.

Reena reached the edge of the bed. The sound was coming from her father’s nose!

Seeing this, she started to laugh a lot. Hearing Reena laugh, her father suddenly woke up and the sound disappeared. Oops!
