# Ants

Nisha went to the garden with her grandmother. There were many kinds of trees in the garden.

There were anthills built under the trees.

The grandmother told Nisha that she used to feed ants in her childhood. Nisha felt happy to hear this.

She went back home and took out some dough from the box.

The next morning, she went to the garden with her grandmother again. She put some flour in the anthills under the trees.

The ants started eating the flour.

Seeing this, Nisha and her grandmother were very happy.
