# No More No!

“Mommy, I want wafers.” “No, Pitku. It’s time for lunch.”

“Then give me some toys.” “No. You’ll make a mess again.”

“I’m going to Savi’s to play.” “No!”

“No, no, no, all the time! You’re always saying no!”

“I don’t like you!”

“Okay. So? What should we do?” “I’m going to tell Papa.”

“Papaaa, I want another, nicer mother! Not this one.”

“And I want another, smarter son! Not this one.”

“Will you take a bite, Mommy? ” “I won’t say no!”
