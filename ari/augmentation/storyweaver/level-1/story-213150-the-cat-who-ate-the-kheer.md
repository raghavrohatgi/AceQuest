# The Cat Who Ate The Kheer

Our mother and father made some kheer . A cat smelled it...

and quietly, she came inside. She ate some ﻿kheer but could not hide!

My mother saw the cat and started to scream.

She ran after the cat like I have never seen.

The cat turned around, growled at our mother.

It jumped off the wall. Oh! Our mother could not catch her after all.
