# The Snake

A black snake, a long snake. The snake spreads its hood&nbsp; and runs fast in the woods.

It sits in a circle. It moves like a wave. It crawls here. It crawls there.

It hisses and comes here. It hisses and goes away. It stops for a minute, and then finds its way.

It eats frogs... and rats! It chews and chews... and then it sleeps.
