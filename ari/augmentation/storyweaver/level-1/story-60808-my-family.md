# My Family

My name is Tuk Tuk. I have a large family.

This is my mother. This is my father.

This is my brother Neetu and my sister Meetu is there too. Look...

My grandparents have also come. Dada-Dadi and Nana-Nani ...

My mother has three sisters. They are my ‘ mausis ’!

My father has four brothers. Two of them are older than him and I call them ‘ Tau Ji ’ and two of them are younger and I call them ‘ Chacha Ji ’.

There are my aunts – my two ‘ Tais ’ and my two ‘ Chachis ’. See...

My family has five young children also.

We make a lot of noise when we are all together.

See, what a large family I have!

Oh! Bujo got left out. He wags his tail all day long and says... bow wow!! bow wow!!
