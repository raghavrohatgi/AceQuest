# My Sister and Me

Anu is my sister. She helps me do my homework.

Some days I do not like to do my homework. But Anu makes me sit down.

Anu has a big cycle. She teaches me to ride it.

When I fall she comes running to help me.

Anu also fights with me. Then I get angry.

But after some time she is sorry.

That is why I love my sister Anu.
