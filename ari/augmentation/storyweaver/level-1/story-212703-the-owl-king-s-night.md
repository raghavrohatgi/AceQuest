# The Owl King's Night

The sun sets and the owl king awakes.

He opens his eyes and gives his body a shake.

After moving, he gets ready to eat.

At midnight, he calls all his friends to meet.

The gathering ends, all his friends sing a song.

As the sun comes out, the owl king makes his bed. He soon gets lost in the dreams in his head.
