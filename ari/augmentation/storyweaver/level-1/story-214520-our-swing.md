# Our Swing

Anuj really wanted to play on a swing.

He went to his big sister Mamuni and said to her sadly, ‘there is no swing anywhere nearby, is there?’

She replied and said ‘we can make a swing ourselves!’

Mamuni then went to the roof to get an old tyre and some rope.

She threw the rope onto a strong branch! Anuj stood there observing with curiosity.

Mamuni then tied the rope to the tyre, making sure the knots were tight.

She then said, ‘our swing is ready!’ Anuj was happy…

and the two siblings played on the swing all afternoon!
