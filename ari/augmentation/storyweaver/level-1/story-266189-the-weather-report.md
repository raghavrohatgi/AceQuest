# The Weather Report

Time for the weather report!

Today, the weather is sunny...

and so very hot!

Today the weather is rainy...

and so very wet.

Today it’s cloudy and overcast.

Today it was windy...

and now it’s stormy!

And today? It’s cold.

Oh, today? Today the weather is just right.
