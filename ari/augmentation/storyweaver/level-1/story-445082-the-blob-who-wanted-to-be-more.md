# The Blob Who Wanted to Be More

Blob always wanted to be different, but did not know how.

One day, a storm&nbsp; separated her from&nbsp; her family.

She found herself in a strange new&nbsp; world, yet she rolled&nbsp; on.&nbsp;

The Gillyfangs were the first to reach out. &nbsp; T hey gave her arms.

Waving goodbye, she rolled on.

Next, she bumped into The Teramesos.&nbsp; They gave her new legs.&nbsp;

And so she walked away towards her next adventure.

Then came the&nbsp; Hibibibis.&nbsp; They&nbsp; showered her&nbsp; with beauty and&nbsp; gentleness.

On and on Blob went, making friends along the way.

Now she could walk, run, jump, push!

She bid farewell to all the new friends she had made...

And wondered, "What next?"
