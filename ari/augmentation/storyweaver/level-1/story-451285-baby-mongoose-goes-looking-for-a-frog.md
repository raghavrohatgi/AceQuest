# Baby Mongoose Goes Looking For a Frog

Baby Mongoose is bored of eating cluster figs! She sets out to hunt for a frog. But how does a frog look?

“Hey, are you a frog?” “No! I’m a heron. A frog has four legs.”

“Hey, you have four legs. Are you a frog?” “No! A frog has large eyes.”

“Your eyes are as large as the full moon. I am sure you are a frog!” “No! I am an owl. A frog is green in colour.”

“Ah! You are green in colour. You must be a frog!” “No! I am a grasshopper.&nbsp;A frog has spots.”

“You have spots. Are you a frog?” “I do have spots, but can you see four legs?”

“Four legs… Large eyes… Green colour… Black spots…”

“You are a frog! Am I right?”

“Yes, you are right! I am a frog! And I can jump too.”

“Oh no! I will have to eat cluster figs again today!”
