# I Feel Safe When..

Anku and Soni are ready for school. It is a rainy day in the village. Mummy is worried about them. Her heart is beating fast.&nbsp; ‘Anku! Soni! Walk slowly and be careful today.’&nbsp; ‘ Yes Mummy, do not worry we will take care,’ says Anku. ‘Ritu is also coming with us. We will be careful,’ says Soni.

Ritu meets them outside.&nbsp; ‘Drip-drop drip-drop!’&nbsp; The rain falls on their umbrella. ‘I always feel safe when I am with you two,’ says Soni.

After school Anku, Soni, and Ritu are milking the cows.&nbsp; ‘What happened Ritu?’ asks Anku.&nbsp; ‘I was finishing my homework but Pranav, my younger brother, wanted to play. He asked me again and again. I got very angry at him. My chest felt heavy. Now I feel bad.’&nbsp;

‘Sometimes I feel upset when Anku does not have time for me. Then she tells me the reason and I understand,’ says Soni ‘Soni is right, talk to him. If you tell him why you were busy he won’t be angry,’ says Anku. ‘Thank you Anku and Soni for listening. I feel loved when you listen to me. I can share everything with you two,’ says Ritu.

Rohan never goes to the gaderas * alone.&nbsp; He is scared of falling. His body feels tight and he cannot swim.&nbsp; Mummy and Papa say that he can get hurt because of the big rocks.&nbsp; Today Rohan is with Sunil and Mohan. He is not scared. They swim and play in the river. ‘Splash splash splash!’&nbsp;&nbsp; Rohan feels safe and his body is relaxed when he is with Sunil and Mohan. * Gaderas: small canals at the foot of the mountains

Sunil comes home from school.&nbsp; Mummy has gone to the forest and Papa to work.&nbsp; Sunil waits for them to come back.&nbsp; Sunil does not know when they will come back.&nbsp; He feels alone and sad.&nbsp; His body feels tight and heavy.

Suddenly his dog Tikki comes running at Sunil.&nbsp; 'Bhau Bhau Bhau!' barks Tikki. Sunil feels safe and his body feels light when he is with Tikki.

In the evening Rohan, Sunil, Anku, Soni, and Ritu go to the forest.&nbsp; Rohan brings his goats and donkeys. Sunil brings his cows and Tikki. Anku and Soni come with their cows and Guddu with the rabbit. Ritu comes with a buffalo and goats.&nbsp; They talk, laugh, jump, and play with their animals.&nbsp; The forest has many wild animals but they feel safe together. What makes you feel safe?
