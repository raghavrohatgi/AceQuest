# Cool Cool Ice Gola

Ting ting ting ting. "Shubham, Ira, Ice Gola Uncle is here!"

All the kids rush to Ice Gola Uncle!

We look at each other through the bottles. Our faces look so funny!

Yummmm… Colourful, magical golas!

Saee taps Abhi’s gola.

A gola falls down, but Bhoori is very happy.

I love ice golas. I see them everywhere.

Shubham wants to be a gola uncle when he grows up.

He will make me a HUGE gola.

Shubham can have a bite, and so can Bhoori.

I will gobble up the rest!
