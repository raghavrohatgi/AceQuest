# I Have So Many Friends

I have two friends at school.

Amit and Sujay.

I have many other friends too.

Such as this mango tree.

This earth mover is my friend.

Also, the uncle who sells peanuts.

Meet my new friend, who lives next door.

You know who else is my friend? My Aaji!

These books are my friends too.

They take me to so many places!

Do you have such friends?
