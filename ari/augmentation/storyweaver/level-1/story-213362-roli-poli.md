# Roli-Poli

Father has gone out of the city. That’s why mother has to give Roli-Poli a bath.

When mother went to get the soap, then Roli-Poli started playing in the water.

When mother went to get the clothes, then Roli-Poli started jumping around.

When mother tried to catch them, then Roli-Poli started running here and there!

When mother got upset, then Roli-Poli started cheering her up.

When mother made Roli-Poli wear their pyjamas, then Roli-Poli started giggling.

When mother started to feed them, then Roli-Poli started shouting with hunger.

As soon as their grandparents came, Roli-Poli started showing them love.

When night arrives, Roli-Poli are tired. Then they go to their bed and fall asleep.
