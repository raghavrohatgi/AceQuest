# A Leaf Goes on a Trip

A huge tree, with lakhs of leaves.

Swooooshhhh came the wind. Swishhhh flew the leaves.

A playful leaf floated down to Kareem’s basket.

It met a bulbul.

It raced with a car.

And then it flew in to meet Kabeer.

Kabeer twirled it on his cheek.

He wore it as a crown.

It gently tickled his nose… Aaaaachhooo!

He kept it safely in his favourite book.

And 340 days later…
