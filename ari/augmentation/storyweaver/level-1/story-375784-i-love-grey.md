# I Love Grey

I love grey

with a dash of pink.

I love white

with a scribble here, &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; and a doodle there.

I love orange

with a serving of green.

I love blue

with a streak of yellow.

But most of all, I love black

with a warm, cosy hug.
