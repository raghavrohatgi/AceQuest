# Tara and the Magic Glass

Tara’s best friend is her magic glass

Who makes everything look big. (Oh! So vast.)

Grass is no longer just grass.

Bugs are giants and cats are lions.

"Look at that!” She says and laughs.

“What shall we see tomorrow, my magic glass?”
