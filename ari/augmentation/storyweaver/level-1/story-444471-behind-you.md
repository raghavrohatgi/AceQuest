# Behind You

Mom and Dad have gone to work. “Don’t worry, I’ll clean up the house and cook for the kids,” says May.

May asks, “Where is the broomstick?” “Behind you,” says Dan.

May asks, “Where is the clean sponge?” “Behind you,” says Kim.

May asks, “Where is the hose pipe?” “Behind you,” says Chi.

At the end of the day, the house is still dirty,&nbsp; the dishes haven't been washed and&nbsp;the garden&nbsp;is a mess. “Fine! If you want to play, I’ll play with you,” says May.

The children are tired and hungry after playing. They start to look for May. “Where’s May?”

“Behind you,” says the closet.
