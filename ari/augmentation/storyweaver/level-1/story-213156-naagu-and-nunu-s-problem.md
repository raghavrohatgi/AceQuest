# Naagu and Nunu's Problem

Naagu and Nunu are best friends.

One day, both of them did not want to go to school.

But Nunu’s mother made her understand.

She also put potato ﻿parathas in her tiffin﻿.

Naagu‘s father also made him understand. He filled his tiffin with eggs.

Upset Nunu started walking towards her school.

Naagu’s father had also sent Naagu to school.

They both reached the school but they were afraid to go inside the classroom.

Math questions were troubling both of them.

The teacher elephant knew the secret to their problem. He called to both of them.

He drew pictures like this to explain the maths questions.

After that, both of them explained a lot of maths questions to each other by drawing pictures.

Both of them said, “Hurray! It is so simple to solve maths questions. Today we have understood so much.”
