# Where Is Your School?

I go to school every day.

Chiki and I see many schools on our way.

The parrots fly to a school on a guava tree.

The squirrels run to a school&nbsp; on a mango tree.

The pigeons sit in a school on electric wires

The ducks float in a school on the lake.

Baby cows walk to a school in a field.

Can you see the tamarind tree?

My school is under that tree.

And the crows are above me. In a school on the tamarind tree!

Chiki goes to school on Sundays. In my home!

No books and no schoolbag for her. In the home school, I am her teacher!
