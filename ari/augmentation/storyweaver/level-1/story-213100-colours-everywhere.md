# Colours Everywhere

Babli started playing with colours. She mixed red with yellow. Which colour did it make? Orange!

This time she mixed white with red. Now guess the colour? Pink!

She mixed a little black with red. Which colour did it make? Brown! Wow! That was fun!

Now you try - mix different colours and form new colours! &nbsp;&nbsp;
