# Pinku and Pankhu

This is Pinku and I am Pankhu.

Let me tell you about all the work that I do.

Early in the morning, I wake Pinku up.

When Pinku throws his ball, I go and get that too.

When Pinku dries the ﻿papad , I shoo away the crows.

When Pinku paints a picture, I give him the colours.

When the cat sips the milk, I chase it out.

When Pinku sits to study, I become his pillow.

When Pinku is tired, I am tired too.
