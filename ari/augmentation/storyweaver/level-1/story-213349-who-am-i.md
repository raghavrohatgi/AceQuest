# Who Am I?

I can hop on two legs. I can use my tail. I can jump far. I can run far.

I keep my baby in my pocket. Wherever I stop, my baby comes out and plays a lot!

When my baby gets tired, it comes back in my pocket and sleeps with ease.

Guess, who am I? Think, think! What is my name?&nbsp; Can you guess or should I tell you?&nbsp; Yes! My name is Kangaroo.
