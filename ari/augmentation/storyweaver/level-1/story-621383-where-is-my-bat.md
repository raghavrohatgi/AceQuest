# Where Is My Bat?

I have lost my bat, and I cannot find it.

I looked behind the door. It was not there.

I looked for it on the shelf. I could not find it there.

I looked for it under the bed. Not there either.

I searched inside the box. &nbsp; There was no sign of it.

I searched&nbsp; the attic. I could not find it&nbsp; anywhere. I looked here, there and everywhere, I could not find it anywhere.

Oh! There’s my bat!
