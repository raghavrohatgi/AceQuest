# Train

Chuk-chuk-chuk. It runs on the tracks.

Chak-chak-chak. It stops at the station.

A train travelling miles away.

Engine at the front and coaches at the back.

Train coaches filled with people.

Some people on the train are reading.

Some people are seen talking.

Some are just looking outside the window.

These are the ways everyone travels on a train.
