# Hide and Seek

We have come to the village to meet our grandmother.

Me, my mother, my father and my brother.

Grandmother has a garden so neat, all the fruits there are very sweet!

Sometimes when I am free, I quickly climb and hide on the tree.

Everyone keeps looking around, in the well and in the ground.

But I come down only when, my lovely grandmother calls my name.
