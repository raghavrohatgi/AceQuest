# How Do I Grow from My Mistakes?

Everyone in school is getting ready for Annual Day. Asma Madam works on the decorations. ‘Sunil, you make red and blue flags. Anku, can you make a big poster that reads ‘Annual Day’? ‘Yes Madam!’ says Anku feeling excited.

‘Okay bring it to school tomorrow morning!’&nbsp; says Asma Madam feeling proud of her students. At home Anku paints the poster.&nbsp; Anku works on it all afternoon.&nbsp; She keeps the poster out in the sun for it to dry.

In the evening, Anku comes to take the poster. She finds Guddu the rabbit sitting on it. ‘Oh no Guddu!’ she says.&nbsp; The poster has Guddu’s footprints on it. Anku starts crying. She feels worried. ‘I should not have put it out. What will I tell Asma Madam tomorrow?’ Anku is upset with herself.

After some time Anku stops crying. ‘I made a mistake but I can make the poster again.&nbsp; Ritu and Soni will help me.&nbsp; I should be kind to myself and ask for help,’ thinks Anku. Anku asks Ritu and Soni to help.&nbsp; Together they work faster. The new poster is more colourful and bright.&nbsp; They all feel proud when the poster is complete. ‘Thank you Ritu and Soni,’ Anku says feeling grateful.

Rohan comes home late from school.&nbsp; Mohan is playing with the goats. ‘Bhaiya bhaiya, will you play cricket with me?’ asks Mohan feeling excited. ‘No Mohan, stop asking me everyday! Go play with other friends. I am tired,’ shouts Rohan. Mohan feels surprised and leaves.

Mehh Mehh Mehh &nbsp; Rohan sits with his goats.&nbsp; ‘Oh no! Why did I get so angry at Mohan?’&nbsp; Rohan asks himself and his goats,&nbsp; feeling sad and tired. ‘I am busy but I should have told Mohan that. He must be feeling angry. I don’t want to hurt anyone with my actions. I will talk to him,’ thinks Rohan.

‘Sorry Mohan, I should not have shouted at you. You just wanted to play with me but I got very tired after dance practice,’ says Rohan. ‘It’s okay Bhaiya, I understand,’ says Mohan. ‘After tomorrow we can play cricket every day!’ says Rohan feeling happy. ‘Yayy! We can play tomorrow!’ says Mohan.

On the Annual Day, everyone feels excited. Asma Madam loves Anku’s posters and Sunil’s flags.&nbsp; All the parents have come to school. They enjoy the children’s dance.&nbsp; Anku, Rohan, Sunil, and Ritu feel very proud after the Annual Day.
