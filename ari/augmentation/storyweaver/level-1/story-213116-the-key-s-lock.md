# The Key's Lock

Sandeep found a key under the big trunk.

If there is a key, there should be its lock.

He started to look for the lock of the key.

He placed the key in the lock of the trunk. The key was very loose.

He placed it in the lock of his uncle’s cupboard. The key did not even go inside.

He placed the key in the lock of his mother’s cycle. The key got stuck in it. He tried very hard to take out the key. Finally, the key came out.

Now he placed the key in the lock of his grandmother’s trunk. Click! The lock opened.

Hurray! It is the key to grandmother’s trunk.
