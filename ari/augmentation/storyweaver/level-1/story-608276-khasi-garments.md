# Khasi Garments

Kynjri Tabah&nbsp; Kynthei Mukmor and Dhara Tiewlasubon Pansngait Sai khyllong Mahu Rupa

Kynjri Tabah Kynthei Mahu Rupa

Sai khyllong Tiewlasubon Pansngait

Kpieng Paila Soptipoh and Jymphong Jaiñboh Kynjri Tabah Shynrang&nbsp; and Ryngkap

Ryngkap Kynjri Tabah Shynrang&nbsp;

‘Khasi Garments,’ is one of the three books which is a part of 'The Garment Series.' 'The Garment Series,' is part of the 45 Meghalaya-contextual storybooks curated under The Forgotten Folklore Project (TFFP), an initiative by the Sauramandala Foundation. The TFFP team is grateful to Ms Daptisani Khasain and Mr Pynksan La Kupar Manik Syiem for sharing extensively on the different types of Khasi garments used by the Khasi community; special thanks to Parij Borgohain for sharing images of the garments as well, without which this book would not have been possible.'
