# What A Muddle!

It was morning. Ma was spreading cow dung in the yard. Grandpa was drinking his third cup of tea.

Amol and Nishi were studying hard.

Suddenly a fly came buzzing by. Amol shooed the fly away.

Off flew the fly and sat on Grandpa’s nose.

“Achhoooo!” sneezed Grandpa, loudly. The hot tea fell on Mamoo’s tail.

Mamoo ran to the verandah.

There were paw marks on Nishi’s notebook. “Where did my notebook go?” muttered Amol.

Nishi ran after Mamoo.

Ma slipped in the wet dung and fell.

“My noteboooook!” yelled Amol.

Just one fly, and what a muddle!
