# Alex's Super Medicine

I’m not feeling so good, Mom. Let’s visit Dr Bongi. She can help.

Drink this medicine, Alex. It will make you feel better. But I don’t like medicine. It’s yucky.

Jett, I’ll let you play with my shiny red car if you drink my medicine. Eeew, no way!

How can I make my medicine taste better? I know, I’ll put it on my sandwich.

Maybe I should hide it away. I know, I’ll hide it in the garden.

I’d better try something else. I know, I’ll hide it in a bird’s nest.

Hmm, maybe I should&nbsp; just try it?

Wooah, this is actually so much fun! I feel something... What’s happening?

I feel&nbsp; so much&nbsp; better! And all I had to do was drink my medicine. You did it, Alex! We’re so proud of you.
