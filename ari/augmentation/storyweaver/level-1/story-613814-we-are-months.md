# We Are Months

January is the first month of the year.

Then comes February, telling us “Spring is here!”

March is a month that is so very windy,

But April brings rain to keep us all busy.

May is for picnics under skies so blue,

June has sunny days, rainy days and happy rainbows too.

July is for dancing in the rain,

In August you can do it all over again.

September skies are for starry wishes,

October is for catching a bucketful of fishes.

November’s cherry blossoms bring in good cheer,

And December is the last month of the year.

Every month is different &nbsp; but together they make up one year.
