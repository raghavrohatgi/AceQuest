# Jalebi

The ﻿ ﻿halvai ﻿ lights a stove. He puts the pan on top.

He cooks some jalebis in the pan, and adds some sugar when he can.

Looking at the sweets, Muniya smiles.

Licks her lips, rolls her eyes.

She goes home to get some money...

and comes back to fill her tummy.
