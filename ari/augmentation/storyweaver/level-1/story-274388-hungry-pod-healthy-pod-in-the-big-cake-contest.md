# Hungry Pod & Healthy Pod in The Big Cake Contest

There was a Cake Contest in Podville. Hungry Pod decided he would enter the contest and make the world’s best cake. He loved to eat but he also loved to cook. YESSSS!!!

Hungry Pod went to the market to buy everything he needed to make a cake. On the way he met Healthy Pod. Hungry Pod went to the market to buy everything he&nbsp;needed to make a cake. On the way he met Healthy Pod. “Hello Healthy Pod,” said Hungry Pod. “I am going to bake a cake for the Cake Contest. You can eat some of my cake when it’s ready.”

“Oh no,” said Healthy Pod. “I never eat cake. I only eat spinach and broccoli. See? That’s why I’m so fit.” “I can eat anything,” said Hungry Pod. “I am always hungry.” Off he went to buy everything.

Hungry Pod reached the market. He bought butter, eggs, sugar, chocolate and flour. But Instead of sugar, he got some salt. It looked the&nbsp;same to him so he didn’t realise what he had done. OOPS!

He went home and began to make the cake. He mixed everything nicely together in a big bowl. Finally he put the cake into the oven. Then he covered it with gooey chocolate. He couldn’t wait to eat it. Yummmm!!!

Hungry Pod decorated his cake. He put colourful sprinkles on it to make it shine. Finally his cake was ready. He carried his cake carefully to the Cake Contest. There were lots of cakes, but Hungry Pod’s cake looked the nicest. Everyone was sure he would win. Hmmm!!!

Fussy Pod was the judge. She took a big bite of Hungry Pod’s cake. “PHOOOOEEEEYYYY!!!” “This cake is terrible,” she said. “It tastes salty, not sweet.” Everyone looked at poor Hungry Pod, who had gone red. He suddenly knew what had gone wrong. He must have put in salt instead of sugar.

Healthy Pod put his arm around Hungry Pod. "Don't Worry, Hungry Pod. Your cake is still the best decorated" Hungry Pod got the prize for the best decorated cake. Hurray!!! CLAP CLAP CLAP CLAP CLAP

He and Healthy Pod went home. Hungry Pod made a new cake. This time he remembered to put sugar. The cake was delicious. Even Healthy Pod, who only eats spinach and broccoli, ate a big, big slice. MMMMM...
