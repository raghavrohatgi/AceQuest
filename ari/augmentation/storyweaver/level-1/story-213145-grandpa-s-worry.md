# Grandpa's Worry

Potato or onion, eggplant or tomato, Whatever grandma finds, she juggles it like a tornado.

A pair of glasses and a watch, an umbrella and a stick. She spins them all around and quick. But grandpa’s heart beats faster with worry, what if she drops something in all this hurry?

Sometimes grandma goes to grandpa’s office and juggles all his things without a single miss.

Now grandpa has the same dream day, night and noon, that grandma juggles the sun, the earth and the moon.
