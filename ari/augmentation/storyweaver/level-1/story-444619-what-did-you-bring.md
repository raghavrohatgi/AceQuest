# What Did You Bring?

Manu, bring me some curry leaves quickly!

Come along with me, Bholu!

Now, what did you see there?

Hello, butterfly! Don’t fly away!

Oh, how beautiful! Ma will love it.

Ma! Look what I have brought for you...

And where are the curry leaves, Manu? Oh no, I forgot!
