# Bunty and Mother's Kite

Bunty's father bought a kite for him.

Bunty kept his kite near the window. It rained heavily during the night.

When Bunty woke up in the morning, he saw that the kite was all wet.

After seeing that the kite is all wet, Bunty becomes very sad.

Then he heard his mother's voice. In her hands were glue, paper and broom sticks.

Mother said - let's make a kite by ourselves! They quickly made a kite.

Bunty wrote on the kite- Bunty and Mother's Kite!
