# The Rat in the Cat

The cat saw a rat.

The rat saw the cat

The cat ran after the rat. The cat ran fast. But the rat ran faster.

The rat went under a mat.

The cat jumped over it and

The rat hid. The cat searched up

The cat looked inside the dustbin

and outside it.

The rat stayed hidden. The cat slept on the mat.

The rat ran.

Fat cat. No rat.
