# The Tortoise and The Rabbit

Two friends lived in a jungle together.&nbsp; One was tortoise and the other was rabbit.

Always friends no matter the weather,&nbsp; food and play they enjoyed together.

One day, the rabbit shared an idea with the tortoise.

The tortoise liked the idea. They both decided to race.

After running for some time, the rabbit stopped to rest.&nbsp; He fell asleep. But the tortoise did not stop.

Suddenly, the tortoise saw a lion. He was scared. He stopped running.

The rabbit woke up. He ran to look for his friend.

The rabbit saw the lion and jumped to save the tortoise.

The tortoise climbed on the rabbit’s back The rabbit started running, saving the tortoise's life.
