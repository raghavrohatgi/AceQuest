# A World of Books

Shreya, Shiva, Saman and Samar, spent all day gathering old objects together.

Without playing or even studying, from exhaustion they ended up sitting.

A woman would see the four of them each day. She would think something and then be on her way.

One day she stopped right there and removed from a box some books to spare.

She put some here and she put some there and began to read one of these books to spare.

Saman went to look at one of the books. Wow! The pictures were very beautiful.

Shreya, Shiva and Samar also picked up some books. They started living through these books.

These conversations with books continued to increase. As this happened they began to also play with ease!
