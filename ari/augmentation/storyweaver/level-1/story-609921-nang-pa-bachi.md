# Nang.pa Bachi

Where did your father go?

To cut wood.

What tree did he cut?

Bolong Agatchi. ﻿

Where did he sell it?

How much did he sell it for?

What did he buy with the money?

Who ate it all up?

Pot-bellied little sister.

About 'Nang·pa Bachi' “Nangpa Bachi Re·anga,” is a sing-song game in which two groups engage in a playful exchange — one posing questions and the other providing answers. The children sing about a father who goes to the market and sells logs. Using the money he earns, he buys dry fish and brings it back home. Regarded as raillery and similar to mini performances, participants joyfully compete to label each other as the “pot-bellied little sister.” Games like these not only entertain but also continue the rich tradition of oral storytelling.

Glossary Bolong agatchi: A type of big and tall tree Goalpara: A district in Assam
