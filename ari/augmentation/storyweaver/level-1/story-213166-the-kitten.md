# The Kitten

Aashi was playing outside the house.

Just then, she heard someone cry in pain. She looked here and there.

There was a small kitten lying on the roadside.

Aashi went near it. The kitten’s leg was hurt. Aashi got scared.

She called her mother. Her mother came out and took the kitten inside the house.

Her mother put medicine on the kitten’s leg and tied a bandage.

Aashi brought some milk in a bowl. Mother took the bowl in her hand and fed the kitten. As soon as it drank the milk, it fell asleep on mother’s lap.

From that day onwards, the kitten started living with Aashi.
