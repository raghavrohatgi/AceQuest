# In the Land Where Beetles Rule

In the land where beetles rule Feet may not touch the ground.

In the land where mice are men Huge hares hunt the hound.

In the land of snowy owls Night and day are turned around.

In the land of nightingales No other voice may sing or sound.

In the land where humans rage Everyone goes underground.

In the land where fish are first Oceans eat up all the ground.

In the land where the full moon reigns Bats and acrobats astound.

In the land where the spotted deer live

Axes are barred, trees abound.
