# Aaba

“Aai, the car is here! Aaba is here!”

Mama, Mami and Leela are here too!

Aai makes tea. She adds a spoonful of cream to Aaba’s tea.

Aaba dunks crispy khari puffs into his tea.

He gulps down the tea and wipes his fluffy white moustache.

He smiles. His smile is hiding under his moustache!

Aai smiles too. Her smile does not hide. It makes her cheeks go pink.
