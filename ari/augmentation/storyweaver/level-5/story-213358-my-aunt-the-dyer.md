# My Aunt the Dyer

My aunt the dyer lights her stove.

She starts dyeing lots of clothes.

One blue turban. One yellow sheet.

One red sari. One green cap.

One black pyjama.

One yellow shirt.

My aunt the dyer dyes all the clothes.
