# Raju and Muniya

The spinning top is green and blue.

Muniya’s skirt is yellow.

Raju’s bed is blue.

The spinning top spins&nbsp; round and round.

Muniya dances on the ground.

Raju jumps up and down.

Looking at them, mother smiles.

And joins them as they dance in style!
