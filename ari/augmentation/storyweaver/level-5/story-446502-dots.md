# Dots

“Aaji, what are you drawing?” “Dots.”

“There are so many on your sari.”

“There are two on my forehead.”

“There is one on the baby’s cheek.”

“Aha! Dots on my palms.”

“And there are so many dots here.”

“There are so many dots in the dark too!”
