# The Happy Plant

Aunty gave me a little plant.

I put it by my window.

I woke up in the morning and… Oh no!

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; I gave it a little shower.

When I saw it again after some time…

I said, “Wow, you look so happy!”

Maybe that’s why Mommy gives me a bath every day too!
