# Hats

Funky Monkey came with a box full of hats.

“I want a hat, please,” said Bunny Rabbit. Bunny Rabbit got a pretty red hat.

Ellie Elephant got a colourful hat.

Baby Deer got a soft, fluffy hat.

“Funky Monkey, this hat is for you,” they all said.

Oh no! There goes the last hat!

“Oh, that’s okay. I don’t need a hat!” said Funky Monkey.
