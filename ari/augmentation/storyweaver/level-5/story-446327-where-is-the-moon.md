# Where Is the Moon?

Where did the moon GO?

Yesterday, it was right here. Let’s LOOK for it.

Ma, come, join the SEARCH.

Not there…NOT here too.

Not to be seen at all. So WHERE is it, then?

Ah…I think I KNOW!

The moon was TIRED and is sleeping. It will come AGAIN after resting.
