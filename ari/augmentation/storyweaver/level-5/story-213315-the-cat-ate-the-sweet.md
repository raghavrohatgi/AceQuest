# The Cat Ate The Sweet

There was a fat cat. The fat cat wanted to eat some sweets.

The fat cat looked around.

The fat cat saw a man sitting down.

The man made some sweets.

The fat cat hid and started to eat.

The fat cat saw the man looking its way. The fat cat jumped and ran away.
