# Line

“What are these on our palms, Abu?” Zoya asked.&nbsp; “Lines,” Abu said.

There are lines everywhere. Look at those leaves.

Lines in the green grass, and in the colourful feathers.

In the waves, and in the rain.

In the cobwebs, and wisps of smoke.

“Abu, look, in your kurta!”

Yes, and lines in Zoya’s drawing too!
