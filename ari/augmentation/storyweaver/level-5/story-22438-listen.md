# Listen

Crickets chirp. Chirp Chirp

Mice squeak. Squeak Squeak

Snakes hiss. Hisssss

Dogs bark. Bow Bow

Goats bleat. Baaaaa

Cows moo. Mooo

Lions roar. Rawr

And snails make no sound at all.

Babies babble. ﻿Ga Ga. Goo Goo

And children talk. Blah Blah
