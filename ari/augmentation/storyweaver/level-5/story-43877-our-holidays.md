# Our Holidays

On Sunday , we stayed at home and cleaned.

On Monday , we went to our uncle’s house.

On Tuesday , we went to the local market.

On Wednesday , we went swimming.

On Thursday , we went to the zoo.

On Friday , we went to the fair.

On Saturday , we went for a picnic.
