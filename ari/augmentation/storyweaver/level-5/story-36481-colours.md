# Colours

Bear likes colours.

Now, her ears are yellow.

Now, her tummy is brown.

Now, her mouth is red.

Now, her hands are blue.

Now, her back is green.

Now, her legs are pink.

Now, Bear is colourful!

Oh no! Where did all her colours go?
