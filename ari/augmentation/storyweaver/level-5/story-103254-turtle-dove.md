# Turtle Dove

Turtle Dove can fly.

Turtle Dove cannot swim.

Turtle Dove can eat food.

Turtle Dove cannot chew the food.

Turtle Dove can walk.

Turtle Dove cannot crawl.

Turtle Dove can sit on tree branch.

Turtle Dove cannot hold on to tree branch.
