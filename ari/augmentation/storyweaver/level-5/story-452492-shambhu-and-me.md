# Shambhu and Me

That is our cow, Bhavri. And this is her calf, Shambhu.

Slurp, slurp...I drink milk. Chupu, chupu...Shambhu drinks milk.

Aai milks Bhavri...Chui, chui.

Aaji smears cow dung on the floor...Surr...surr...

Aaji makes cow dung cakes, and sticks them on to the wall. Splat! Splat!

I jump with Shambhu. Up and down, up and down!

I sleep in Aai's lap. Shambhu cuddles up with Bhavri.
