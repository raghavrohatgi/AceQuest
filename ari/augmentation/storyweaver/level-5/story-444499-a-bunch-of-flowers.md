# A Bunch of Flowers

To All Brave Children We hope that through this story, you will understand that your parents also get sick just like you. It is not your job to take care of them when they are ill but hugs and lots of love are always welcome! To All Brave Parents and Caregivers We hope that this book will serve as a springboard to talk about mental health and foster an open conversation with your children.
