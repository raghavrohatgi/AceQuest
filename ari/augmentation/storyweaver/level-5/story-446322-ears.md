# Ears

Kitty’s ears are, oh, so soft.

Jumbo’s ears are, oh, so long.

A rabbit’s ears are, oh, so lovely.

The ears of a mouse are, oh, so small.

My, oh, my, the elephant’s ears are, oh, so big.

Hello, tiny ant. Where are your ears? I can’t see them.

How do you hear, my dear ant? Please come and tell me in my ear.
