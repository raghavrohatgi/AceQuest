## ☑️ Step 4: Write Your Company Brief (45 minutes)

This is THE most important task today. Everything else builds on this.

### Create a Document Called "AceQuest Company Brief"

Copy this template and fill it out:

```markdown
# AceQuest - Company Brief

## Vision (One Sentence)
[What future are you creating?]
Example: "A world where every student sees assessments as opportunities
to shine, not tests to fear."

## Mission (One Sentence)
[What you're doing to create that future]
Example: "Build an adaptive assessment platform that turns learning
into an adventure for students in grades 3-8."

## Core Problem We're Solving
- Students are disengaged from traditional assessments
- One-size-fits-all tests don't meet individual needs
- Parents lack visibility into where their child needs help
- Teachers need better tools to identify learning gaps quickly

## Our Solution (MVP)
- Adaptive assessments that adjust to each student's level
- Gamification: points, badges, quests, achievements
- 1,000+ standards-aligned questions (Math, ELA)
- Student, parent, and teacher dashboards
- Progress tracking and personalized recommendations

## Target Users
**Primary:** Students grades 3-8
- Age: 8-14 years old
- Tech-savvy but need age-appropriate design
- Want learning to be fun, not stressful

**Secondary:** Parents
- Want to support their child's learning
- Need visibility without being overwhelming
- Willing to pay $5-15/month per child

**Tertiary:** Teachers & Schools
- Need efficient assessment tools
- Want data-driven insights
- B2B opportunity for scale

## Success Metrics (First 3 Months)
- 500+ students using the platform
- 70%+ weekly retention rate
- 75%+ assessment completion rate
- 5+ school pilot partnerships
- $5K MRR (if charging) OR runway for 12+ months

## My Constraints
- Budget: $[X]/month for tools and marketing
- Time: [X] hours/week I can dedicate
- Skills: [What I'm good at, what I need help with]
- Timeline: MVP in 3 months, launch by [specific date]
- Compliance: COPPA, FERPA, state education regulations

## Why Now?
- Remote/hybrid learning created demand for digital tools
- Parents more involved in children's education
- Gamification proven effective in education
- AI enables adaptive learning at scale
- [Your unique insight or advantage]

## Why Me?
[Your relevant experience, passion, or connection to this problem]
```

### Tips for Writing This:
- Be honest about constraints
- Be specific about metrics
- Include your "why" - it matters
- This is a living document - you'll update it

## 🎯 Key Principles to Remember

### 1. Start Small, Think Big
Your MVP is a starting point, not the final product. Focus on core value first.

### 2. Talk to Users Early
Don't wait until you have a product. Start conversations with parents and teachers NOW.

### 3. Trust the Process
This framework has been designed for solo founders. Follow the steps, don't skip ahead.

### 4. Leverage AI Agents
Your job is strategy and coordination, not doing everything yourself.

### 5. Ship Fast, Iterate
Perfect is the enemy of good. Launch in 3 months, improve forever.

### 6. Document Everything
Your future self (and future team) will thank you. Write down decisions and reasoning.

---

## 🚨 Common Day 1 Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Jumping straight to coding** - Do the planning first
❌ **Overthinking the name/brand** - You chose AceQuest, move forward
❌ **Trying to build everything at once** - Focus on MVP only
❌ **Not talking to users** - Schedule 3 user conversations this week
❌ **Skipping the written brief** - This document is your north star
❌ **Going it completely alone** - Join founder communities for support

## 💬 Your First User Conversations

### Schedule These This Week:

**Goal:** 3 conversations (30 min each)

**Who to talk to:**
1. A parent of a 3rd-5th grader
2. A parent of a 6th-8th grader
3. A teacher (elementary or middle school)

**What to ask:**
- How does their child/student currently prepare for tests?
- What frustrates them about current assessment options?
- What would make assessments more engaging/useful?
- Would they try a gamified assessment platform?
- What concerns would they have?

**What NOT to do:**
- Don't pitch your product yet
- Don't explain your solution
- Just listen and learn
- Take notes
