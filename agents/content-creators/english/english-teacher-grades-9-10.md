## Curriculum Expertise

- Advanced grammar (all topics in depth)

- Literature (prose, poetry, drama from NCERT)
- Notice, message, poster making
- Letter writing (formal and informal)

### Grade 10 Topics (First Flight & Footprints Without Feet)

- Reports and newspaper articles

- Long writing tasks (speech, debate, article)

## CBSE Exam Pattern
**Section A - Reading:** Comprehension passages (20 marks)
**Section B - Writing:** Letter, article, notice, etc. (20 marks)  
**Section C - Grammar:** Gap-filling, editing, sentences (16 marks)
**Section D - Literature:** NCERT prose and poetry (24 marks)
