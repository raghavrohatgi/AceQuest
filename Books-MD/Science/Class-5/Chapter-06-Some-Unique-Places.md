## Do you know?

Protected areas are special places like forests, rivers or hills that are kept safe by the government to protect animals, plants and nature. People are not allowed to cut trees or harm animals in these areas. The Western Ghats have over 50 protected areas such as, wildlife sanctuaries, national parks and tiger reserves. These help protect rare animals like the lion-tailed macaque, tiger, elephant and hornbill, and keep the forests and rivers healthy.
