Examine the soil more carefully. What small things do you notice in it?

Soil is the topmost layer of the Earth’s surface. Soil is made from rocks that have broken up into tiny pieces, as well as old leaves, roots, stems and living and dead animals like insects.

![img-22.jpeg](img-22.jpeg)

Soil has many insects and other creatures living in it! Some you can see, and some are too small to see. On the top layer of soil and moving around between the grass and leaves you might find insects like ants, termites, small beetles and grasshoppers.
