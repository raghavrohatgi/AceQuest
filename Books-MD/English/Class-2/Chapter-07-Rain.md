# This is My Town

This is my town.
In my town,
There are streets.
In my street,
There are houses.
In my house,
There are rooms.
In my room,
There is a bed.
On the bed,
There is a basket.
In the basket,
There are some flowers.
Flowers in a basket.
Basket on the bed.
Bed in the room.
Room in the house.
House in the street.
Street in the town.
