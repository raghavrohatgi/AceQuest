# Note to the teacher

- Take an A4 size paper.
- Fold the paper lengthwise.
- Fold it again.
- Open it, and bring the corners to the centre.
- Fold one of the lower flaps upward.
- Do the same with the other flap on the opposite side.
- The hat is ready.
