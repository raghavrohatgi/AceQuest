# A Farm

My dear grandpa had a farm
E-I-E-I-O
And on that farm,
He had a cow
E-I-E-I-O
With a moo-moo here
And a moo-moo there
Here a moo, there a moo
Everywhere a moo-moo
My dear grandpa had a farm
E-I-E-I-O

## C. Talking in pairs

Child 1: What is the colour of a parrot?

Child 2: A parrot is _______________

Child 1: What is the colour of a crow?

Child 2: A crow is _______________

Child 1: What is the colour of a rat?

Child 2: A rat is _______________

Child 1: What is the colour of a peacock?

Child 2: Oh! It has many colours.
