Make a $3 \times 4$ grid for playing 'Bingo'.

Fill the boxes of the grid with any number from 2 to 12. Numbers can be repeated.

Let one child throw two dice and say the sum of the numbers of dots on the dice. Other children will cross the number on their grid. Continue this till child crosses all the numbers. Bingo!

Encourage children to develop their strategy for selecting the numbers to be written on the grid.
