# Touch and Tell. Who am I?

Collect different objects with variation in faces, corners and edges. Blindfold the child and ask them to pick an object as per the details given by other children. For example, a child may be asked to take out an object with one corner. Similarly, continue with other objects.

# Project Work

Take a cardboard sheet and try to make an object with four faces.

Now, try to make an object with one face.

Encourage children to discuss and find out the objects which have one, two, three or no corners. They may make such objects using clay or dough.
