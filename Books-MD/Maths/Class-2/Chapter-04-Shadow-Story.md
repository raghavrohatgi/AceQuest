Shadow Story (Togalu)

Wow! These shadows are so amazing.

![img-0.jpeg](img-0.jpeg)

Nakul and his friends saw a shadow play known as Togalu Gombeyaata in a village fair. They all were fascinated to see the shadows.

They also wanted to make shadows. So, they made different animal shadows on the wall with their fingers. You can also try to make the shadows.

![img-1.jpeg](img-1.jpeg)

Share information about Togalu Gombeyaata with children which is a puppetry art form practised in Karnataka. The puppets are used to depict scenes from Indian epics. Let children also share their experiences of watching a puppet show.
