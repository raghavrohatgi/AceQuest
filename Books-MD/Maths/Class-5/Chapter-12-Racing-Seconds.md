Do you notice the use of a new unit 'seconds' in the picture?

**1 min = 60 seconds**

In situations like a race, ‘seconds’ help us observe small differences in time taken by participants. Each participant took 1 minute but how much more? Identify the child who won the race. How much time did the child take?

# Conversion of Hours to Minutes

Zainab and Remo were converting time from hours to minutes and vice-versa.

![img-22.jpeg](img-22.jpeg)

We know 1 hr = 60 min.
So, 2 hr will have
$2 \times 60 = 120$ min.

This means that 180 min = 3 hr.
As 3 hrs = 3 times 60 = 180 min.
