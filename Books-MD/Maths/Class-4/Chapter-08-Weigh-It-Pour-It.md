Let Us Find

a) How many 500 ml bottles will fill a 1 l bottle?

![img-32.jpeg](img-32.jpeg)

$$
500 \text{ ml} + 500 \text{ ml} = 1 \text{ l}
$$

$$
1000 \text{ ml} = 1 \text{ l}
$$

$$
500 \text{ ml} = \frac{1}{2} \text{ l}
$$

![img-33.jpeg](img-33.jpeg)

![img-34.jpeg](img-34.jpeg)

$$
250 \text{ ml} + 250 \text{ ml} + 250 \text{ ml} + 250 \text{ ml} = 1 \text{ l}
$$

$$
1000 \text{ ml} = 1 \text{ l}
$$

$$
250 \text{ ml} = \frac{1}{4} \text{ l}
$$
