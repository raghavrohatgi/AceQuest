How Do Volcanoes Erupt?
0:00 / 3:42
⛶
A volcano is an opening in Earth's surface where molten rock, gases, and ash can escape from below the surface. When pressure builds up inside the Earth, it forces magma upward through the vent. The dramatic explosion we see is called an eruption. Volcanoes can be found on land and under the ocean.
